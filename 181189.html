This Foster Kitten Made the Internet Fall in Love With Her Smile

She has suffered a lot, even though she has just begun her life. The little one is still alive. Lauren couldn’t resist photographing her because of the happy smile that Blossom gave her.

After the photos were uploaded by Boutz, it was found that the smile of Blossom was also contagious. The internet caught up with her. More than 72K people liked the photos and more than 217K shared them on Facebook.

Lauren works for the City of Albuquerque Animal Welfare Department. The Powerpurr Girls are in the photos. Buttercup, Bubbles, and Blossom. They are about 5 weeks old.
