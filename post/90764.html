Housewife's plaque removing trick will keep you from visiting the dentist

As we grow up, we all face the most embarrassing teeth problem, i.e. plaque. It is something that everyone with teeth gets. And we usually want to get rid of it as soon as possible. But, not every one of us can afford to spend lots of bucks to visit a dentist and get the plaque removed. Oh! So is it gonna remain forever?

No, not really if you try out this grandma’s trick. We all believe that our elderly have unlimited life hacks that actually work. And one such tricks include the one to get rid of this yellowish plaque. And this surely works if you do it the way we tell you. So scroll down to look at the easy-peasy steps of the hack.

Once you use this trick tonight, you’ll definitely see the results soon.

What all you need for this trick?

  1. Salt: Take about a teaspoon of salt and put it in a small glass or mug.
  2. Baking Soda: Mix about two teaspoons of baking soda with the salt.
  3. Water: Slowly, add a teaspoon of water in the mixture. The paste should be firm enough to put on your toothbrush.

And that’s all for the ingredients. And surely these ingredients are cheap and also easily available.

Directions of use to get rid of plaque

Brush your teeth with this homemade paste for at least three minutes. Then rinse it with water. That’s all that you need to do. Also, if you want to stay free from plaque, we’recommend you use this trick at least twice or thrice in a month.

Tip: Avoid the use of this paste on tongue as it is salty and bitter.
