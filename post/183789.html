Dad Left Shattered When Daughter Chooses Biological Father Over Him, Refuses to Walk Her Down the Aisle After ‘Real Dad’ Passed Away

A dad has not spoken with his daughter in years. He wrote a post on r/AITA that received about  21.2k upvotes.

The family collapsed after he divorced his wife, and his daughter started pulling away from him.

The situation has changed and the author’s daughter reached out to him after years of asking if he could walk her down the aisle. The dad turned to the internet to find out if it was the right decision.

 

The father received a request to walk his daughter down the aisle, but he refused.

 

He had a complicated relationship with his daughter, who turned away when she found out he was not her father.
