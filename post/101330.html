My Comics Are Inspired By My Dog And Most Dog Owners Will Relate To Them (34 New Pics)

If you love dogs a little more just like the rest of us, you really can’t miss out on the popular “Hey Buddy Comics”. This is one of the best comics that you can ever come across, which perfectly shows what it is like to have a dog. This comic captures everything from silliness, emotion, heartbreak to cuteness. 

I have been writing this comic for so long, probably from the time when my big furry friend was just a cute little puppy. All I ever wanted was to communicate with my buddy but that is not possible (obviously). So I just imagine the conversation that we two would have and have started this comic. 

Scroll down and let these amazing conversations melt your heart.
