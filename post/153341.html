Artist Who Makes People Cry With Her Animal Comics Just Released A New Tragic One About A Freezing Dog

While most comic artist makes us smile with their art, Jenny Jinya is known for making people cry with her art. With her Loving Reaper comics, she had made so many people cry in the past. Now, she is back with her other comic strip. 

Over time, she has included some more animals in her work. And a few days earlier, she has uploaded a strip that takes us back to winter. If you want to see it, scroll down and have a look.
