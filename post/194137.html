30 People Who Had To Live With The Worst Roommates, And Shared Pictures

Have you ever shared your house or flat with another person? If you have, you would know that living with roommates can be a real nightmare.

But if you are unfortunate, you might get stuck with someone who thinks that cleaning up is optional and that a little mold is nothing to make a big fuss about. And sometimes it’s your own family members that can become these terrible roommates.
