Meet Chowder, The Dog Who Looks Like A Giant Teddy Bear (21 Pics)

Ok so let’s play a game. Tell us what’s the cutest dog you have ever seen? Have you picked one? Now do a quick search and find Chowder, the name of a dog. So what’s your answer? Yes, we know, it’s Chowder. And why shouldn’t he be the cutest? After all, he looks like a giant teddy bear (of course, that can walk, eat, play, and bark). In a nutshell, Chowder can be explained as a fluffy and adorable teddy bear that has come to life.

Chowder is a six-year-old Chow Chow pup that every pet parent wants to have. This doggo lives in Malina, Philippines, and is super cute. We know you might be thinking that Chow Chow are temperamental dogs. And that is why Chowder has his presence on social networking sites. The humans of Chowder want to show the world that this dog breed is super cute and super sweet. And if given proper care, they will never be violent.
