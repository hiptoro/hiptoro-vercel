This Ice Cream Shop Is Winning The Funny Sign Game (21 Pics)

Have you ever been to B&G Milkyway? It’s an ice cream shop chain in Sioux Falls, South Dakota. A Reddit user SoDakZak who lives in one of the areas where this ice cream shop is located always reads the sign to make his day. 

“I loved reading their funny quips or puns or shower thoughts and decided to start taking pictures of them when I stopped there for a walking taco for my lunch breaks or when my wife and I decide to grab our favorite Avalanche for an evening date!” SoDakZak told us.
