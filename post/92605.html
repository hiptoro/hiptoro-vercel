Woman pours water over neighbor's son after he poured water over her cat

Recently a woman named Shannon Cooper, poured water over her neighbor’s son after he did the same to her cat. She has gone viral after Tweeting about the incident all over the internet.

After sharing her post she ignited a Twitter debate about who was in the right. Cooper has revealed why she threw a basin of water over her young neighbor after he had poured a cup of water over her pet cat.

She explained-

“My 10 year old neighbour just threw a cup of water over my cat who was sitting on the fence minding his own business n laughed so I threw a basin of water over him from the window and now his dad is at my door going mental but I don’t see the problem, don’t touch my cat.”
