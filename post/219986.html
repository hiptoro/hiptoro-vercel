'ALWAYS do your research': Woman Finds the 'Cute Guy' She Matched With On Tinder On Police's Most Wanted List for Stalking

Every week they share the most wanted list,

It looks like the guy whose name was written as “Mitch” is a stalker. Sigler says she saw the profile and knew the picture from the Facebook page for her local police.
