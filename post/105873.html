Dog Owners Will Relate To These 21 Situations

Hello everyone! My name is Erez Zadok. I am a skilled comic book artist and illustrator. I make hilarious dog comics that every pet parent can easily relate to. I try to create simple but funny illustrations on how to raise a dog with my furry friend Joya. 

I have a weekly comic series i.e. Bundle Of Joya. In this comic series, you can find everything from how to raise a dog, the mess they create to the selfless love they shower. This daily adventure of raising a dog is really very exciting and you will like them all. 

If you want to see more comics from Bundle Of Joya, you can follow the page on Instagram. But till then, you can just scroll down to see how life takes a 360-degree flip when you bring a dog.
