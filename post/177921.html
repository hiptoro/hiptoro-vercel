Adele Bursts Into Tears After Being Surprised By Her Teacher

It’s hard to ignore the fact that artists seem to be in a different world from ours, but many will do a lot to show them that they haven’t started doing it yet.

Some tell us about their old habit of dying hard, others say they are the same person they started with, no matter how different they were.

But if there’s one connected feeling that our favorite stars really tell us, it’s them too, once dreamed of as children.

 

On November 22, ITV aired a special concert of the stars that Adele performed in earlier this month.

According to People, the event’s name was Guest with Adele and one of the stars in attendance was Emma Thompson. Emma Thompson asked the singer if anyone had inspired her since she was a child.

She told that she had had a teacher named McDonald, who developed her interest in English and literature and also conducted dances in the cafeteria.

As Adele said, “She’s beautiful. She cares about us and taught us how to care.”

 

However, the two are lost contact after leaving school when the singer was 12 years old.

But what Adele quickly saw was the fact that the professor didn’t stop thinking about her as Thompson revealed that she was in the audience that night.

As you can see, Adele was overcome with emotion as soon as she learned that she could meet her favorite teacher.

 

She couldn’t stop crying when her teacher joined her on the stage.

After McDonald told Adele how proud she was of her, she revealed that this was all planned for her. Adele said she was a little too young since they last saw her.

Adele gave her a second hug as she started pulling again, and McDonald’s said, “Don’t cry, thank you. Thank you for remembering me.”

 

But, as Adele admits, the singer will have a hard time forgetting McDonald because she believes she has changed her life.
