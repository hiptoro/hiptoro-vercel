Photographer Reveals What Hides Under Tattooed People’s Everyday Clothes

People in different parts of the world have a different approach to life. Those, who are fond of getting a tattoo on their body can never stop once they have got one tattoo. London-based photographer Alan Powdrill proves in his new Covered photo series that people who get tattooed come in different shapes and sizes.

Each photograph that you will see below has a comment from the sitter, describing their story. Some have been getting tattoos from their teenage years, while some started in their 40-50’s.
