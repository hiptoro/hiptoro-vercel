Instead Of Covering Grey Roots, This Hairdresser Makes Clients Embrace It With His Powerful Transformations (35 Pics)

Colorist Jack Martin’s California is helping his clients to accept their grey’s and in fact embracing them as well. Jack started this almost three years ago when a client walked into his salon and told him that she had been dyeing her grey hair to brown with a home-coloring box. The lady told Jack that she has to frequently perform this procedure and was rid of it.

Jack took it as a challenge to help his client. Both started discussing ideas, and finally, they came up with a solution. Instead of coloring the hair in brown or black, he opted for sliver color to highlight the natural gray and embrace it. The results were amazing, and since then, Jack has been everyone with the same issue with beautiful transformations.
