UPS Driver Captures The Cutest ‘Clients’ He Meets On Routes, And People Can’t Get Enough (30 Pics)

Hey there! 

You look so sad. What happened? Are you missing your daily dose of awesomeness? Well, in that case, you gotta check this amazing social media account “awww”. This is actually a great community for dog lovers. This community was actually started for UPS drivers so that they can share the cute videos and pictures of the adorable dogs they meet on the way. 

If you want to see something amazing, you should surely check out this social media page. But till then, you can scroll down to see the pictures that we have found just for you. This post especially features a UPS driver, named, Scott Hodges who is madly in love with pit bulls. 

Actually, Hodges used to have a pitbull named Sheb. Unfortunately, she passed away in 2004 at the age of 15. But luckily, Hodges has been working as a UPS driver for nearly 32 years and he gets to see so many cute pitties o his way. Not just pit bulls but he also sees many other cute animals on his way to work.
