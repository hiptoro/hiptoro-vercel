Zoo Employee Reveals 16 Most Interesting Animal Facts She Learned At Her Job And We Can’t Get Enough

The docent at Columbus Zoo and Aquarium recently revealed some fun facts about animals that you might have no awareness about.  He is an educator and so, he likes to tell the visitors of the zoo some reality about animals they come to see. To know more about what she has learnt all these years working for the zoo and how she attends visitors, the docent told Hiptoro, “We are there to answer questions about the animals, talk to the guests about the zoo’s many conservation projects, and assist them any way we can – sometimes just by helping them find the restroom!”.

She also told us, that her job is great and she loves it, “It’s one of my favourite things in life. It’s rewarding to share information with guests and help them have a great experience at our zoo, which we’re very proud of, as it’s considered one of the best zoos in the country!”

When we asked what is required for the training to be a docent, she replied “Our training is thorough and I am always continuing to learn new things. We have weekly meetings for continuing education, and listening to the keeper’s talk (as well as more experienced docents) is always educational for me.”
