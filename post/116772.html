Grumpy Husky Hilariously Doesn’t Want to Play Santa’s Reindeer In Photoshoot

We all love to dress our fur babies in cute Christmas costumes. But what we don’t know is our furry angels hate it. They don’t like wearing anything fancy because all they want to do is eat, sleep, and repeat.  

For starters, here’s a hilarious husky, named Anuko, who doesn’t want to play the reindeer games. He is done with the festive antlers adorning his head and fake costumes. And his expression clearly says this thing.
