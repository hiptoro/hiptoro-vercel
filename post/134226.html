People Are Sharing The Most Bizarre Things They’ve Seen On Public Transport (35 Tweets)

Life has changed since the pandemic hit all of us. But prior to that, millions of people used public transport to travel to their destination. Sharing the space with strangers while you have reached your station is an experience that is weird yet interesting. It involves meeting individuals that you get inspired by, people who you never wanna be friends with, listening to conversations that you have no link with, and lots more.

Recently, James Felton, a comedy writer went on his Twitter handle to ask his followers, ‘What’s the weirdest thing you saw on your commute’? To this tweet, many people had the most natural things to share about their everyday experiences. So, let’s take a look at what they had to say.
