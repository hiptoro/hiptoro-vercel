People Are Posting Their Dog’s Happiest Expressions For The ‘Smiling Dog’ Challenge (30 Pics)

Do you want to take part in the new viral challenge with your furry friend? If yes, all you need to do is say cheese, click a picture and you are done. This amazing challenge has started by the Dog Spotting Society Facebook group and nearly 20k people have participated in this challenge. OMG! 

The #SmilingDogChallenge is both soul-soothing and cute because the happy furry angels look no less than a blessing. If you want to get your stressed heart melt by something awesome, just see the pictures of dogs posted by their owners. 

Scroll down and have a look at these happy good bois and girls.
