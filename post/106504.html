These Dog Playing Cards Have The Most Epic Illustrations On Them

We all love dogs. If we could get everything around us customized in dog theme, we would simply love it. What if, we told you that you can now play your favorite card games with a deck of cards with tiny doodled dogies on it? Isn’t that exciting?

San Franciscan artist John Littleboy operates a creative online playing card shop known as Artiphany.  He is the creator behind these attractive and cute deck of cards.

“Transformed by an artist’s imagination, these ordinary playing cards are a small box of treasure,” he writes about the canine-inspired set on his website

Simple and adorable sketched cards and small boxes of treasure have been john Littleboy’s specialty since 2010.

So, take a look at the deck of cards, and you might never feel good about your ordinary play cards ever again.
