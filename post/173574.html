Hooters Backtracks After Employees Go Viral For Complaining About ‘Disturbing’ And ‘Sexist’ New Uniforms

Wearing uniforms is an unspoken way of telling about a company that the people work for. But will you wear the uniform if it’s disturbing and sexist? Of course, not.

Well, this similar thing has happened with the employees of Hooters. After their uniform was changed, they started complaining about it because of safety and practical reasons.

The restaurant chain Hooters recently updated their employees’ uniforms and they were not happy about them, so the company made them optional

Quite a lot of Hooters Girls were showing the shorts of the new uniform on TikTok and those videos were going viral

Most of the girls were not happy about the changes and weren’t fans of the idea of wearing what they called ‘underwear’ to work

Luckily, the company heard their employees and made a statement saying that the women will be able to choose to wear the uniform that makes them more comfortable

Hooters were always criticized for having their employees wear overly revealing outfits, but this time, it seems they crossed the line

People online were mostly sympathizing with women who thought that Hooters can’t force them to work in underwear
