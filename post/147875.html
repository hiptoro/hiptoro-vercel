16 Men Who’d Do Anything for the Pets They “Never Wanted”

If there is one member in the house who is fond of pets, there is another who would not agree to have pets at first. usually, parents are a bit spectical about having pets at home, but eventually, when you end up getting one, they are the one’s spending most amount of time with them. So, here are some people sharing stories about their partners or parents not wanting to have pets, but eventually fell in love with them.

1. “My dad who ’didn’t want a cat’ showing Lucas every item from his recent grocery trip because ’he wants to see what we’ve got.’”

2. “My dad was really sad when my sister and I moved out but categorically refused to get a dog. My mom got one anyway. This is them now.”

3. “Just another dad surrounded by the pets he never wanted”

4. “No dogs allowed in bed. Exceptions were made.”

5. “From a dad who did not want a pet to a pet who does not want a dad…”

6. “Who does he think I am? His father? I think so.”

7. Dad: “No, I don’t want pets, who will take care of them when we’re on vacation?”

Also dad: *shares his salmon*

8. “’Why would you ever want a rodent in your house?’ My dad said almost 4 years ago when we got our chinchilla. Now he takes every chance he gets to watch TV with her.”

9. “This is my ’what do you want a cat for, I’m allergic?!’ friend. Now he runs around barking like a dog when the cat is bullied by other cats in the garden. So she waits for him until she hears his car.”

10. Dad: “I don’t want a pet, so stop pestering me about it.” Also Dad: “Please go on vacation so I can hang out with my grandson again.”

11. “My dad had to catsit for me last week. He refuses to get another pet. The whole sunroom was converted into a cat playground when I came to pick him up.”

12. “I caught him hugging the giant dog he didn’t want. He was also singing the dog his own personal song.”

13. “He refused to stand up because ’she’d had a long day and needed the nap.’”

14. “From ’it’s not a real dog’ to dancing together…”

15. “From ’we don’t need a rat in the house’ to ’don’t forget his pillow, the cart is too cold for him.’”

16. “My dad reading the news with ’that dog’”
