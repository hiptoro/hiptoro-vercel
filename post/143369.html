35 Women Post Unedited Pics To “Normalize Normal Bodies”

You don’t realise, but a lot of pressure is created on both men and women to look picture perfect. But thanks to these women who came out as they are. Without any professional lighting or photo edits, these women shared their normal photographs. We hope that you all reading this post have confidence in yourself and accept your normal bodies.
