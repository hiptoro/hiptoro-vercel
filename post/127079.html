This Artist Creates Funny Minimalist Comics With No Dialogue (20 Pics)

If you are an artist, you often have to work hard and think about what will make people interested in your work. There are very few people who can make people laugh and enjoy your work by taking very little efforts.

Karlo Ferdon is a Chilean cartoon artist who proves that you don’t need lots of words to make people laugh. He creates “silent” comics about all sorts of comical everyday situations. These comics will definitely put a smile on your face.
