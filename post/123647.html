50 Maine Coon Cats That Will Make Your Cat Look Tiny

You must have heard about the cat breed Maine Coon. They are believed to be the biggest and longest domesticated feline breed in the world. In fact, in 2010, the cat breed held the Genies Book of World Record for being the longest cat in the world. The cat’s name was Stewie, who measured 48.5 in (123 cm) from the tip of his nose to the tip of his tail. An average male Manie Coon cat weighs about 35 lb (15.9 kg).
