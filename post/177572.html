Birthday Tacos Are The New Trend Replacing Cakes

Some people love birthday tacos instead of birthday cake.

Let’s check out. what would be a birthday taco look like? 

Bet you didn’t see this coming.

 

View this post on Instagram

 

A post shared by LAS BRASAS TACOS & GRILL (@las_brasas_tacos)

These are birthday tacos, and they are replacing birthday cakes.

They were pioneered by a Houston-based restaurant.

 

View this post on Instagram

 

A post shared by LAS BRASAS TACOS & GRILL (@las_brasas_tacos)

Las Brasas Tacos & Grill in Houston, Texas offers birthday tacos in the shape of your imminent year. And toppings according to your choice.

Their customers absolutely love them.

 

View this post on Instagram

 

A post shared by LAS BRASAS TACOS & GRILL (@las_brasas_tacos)

And their prices are reasonable, too. You can get 50 tacos for $75 or $100 for $150, which is a total steal! That’s so much taco for your buck.
