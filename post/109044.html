This Shiba Inu Dog Loves Smiling, Especially After Seeing Food

Hello dog lovers, we are back with some super cute images of our furry buddy. Uni is a 9-month-old Shiba Inu living in Tokyo, Japan, and he has a lot to smile about. He is full of life pet and is loved by his owners very much. He is a happy dog in general, but do you want to know which one thing makes him most excited. Can you guess? Delicious food is his weakness.

That’s right, the puppy can’t stop smiling whenever there’s something delicious in front of him, Whether it is a smoothie or a bowl of pasta on the table belongs to his human. He will have the biggest smile on his face.
