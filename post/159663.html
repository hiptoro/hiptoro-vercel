Person Shares A Conversation With An Aggressive Vegan Who Accused Them Of Appropriation Just Because They Enjoy Tofu

A guy on the Internet is self-proclaiming to be the guardian of tofu. Thus, he is ready to pick a fight with anyone who is not vegan but eating tofu. 

This so-called guardian of tofu started cyberbullying a beef-eater, who adored the tofu-lovers ‘tofurkey’ (tofu turkey) recipe. The tofu gatekeeper said, “tofu is strictly for vegans.” 

After exchanging a few texts, the messages were uploaded to the ‘Gatekeeping’ subreddit. Here people share stories and pictures “when someone takes it upon themselves to decide who does or does not have access or rights to a community or identity. /r/gatekeeping is a subreddit for screenshots and stories of gatekeepers in the wild.”

What started as a friendly chat quickly devolved into accusations

The vegan was angry that the meat-eater was eating all of their tofu. Good heavens, the horror

Internet users reacted to the tofu gatekeeper with scorn
