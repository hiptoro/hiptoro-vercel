Artist Creates Comics That Are Funny, Yet Sad and Twisted At the Same Time (20 New Pics)

Many would think that comics featuring bunnies, cupcakes, teddy bears, or flowers must be cheerful. Not in Ryan Pagelow’s case. Ryan Pagelow is the one who created the popular webcomic series called “Buni”.

Buni, Ryan is described as a naive optimist with terrible luck and as someone who is constantly being crushed by the real world. He keeps wishing for the best.

There are some cute comics with not-so-cute endings that Ryan has created.
