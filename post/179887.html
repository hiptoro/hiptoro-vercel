20 Amazing Illustrations That Tear Through Stereotypes Made for Women

Many people in our society still tell women what to do with their bodies and lives. Those people were shut down by the creation of powerful illustrations that focused on many of the common prejudices women face every day. The themes of mental health, career choices, solidarity, body positivity, and many other women’s issues are dealt with by the artist.

Women are encouraged to live life on their own terms, no matter how society tries to shape their behavior and preferences. Her illustrations convey the message of empowerment and self-acceptance.
