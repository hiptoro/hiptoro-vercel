Person Has Their Parents Watch Their Cat During Their First Year Of College, Parents Secretly Give It Away

Sometimes, our parents do something that is really hard for us to accept. Like what the parents of  Reddit user nexerxe did is really hard for them to accept. This user has left the home for pursuing higher education. Unfortunately, they couldn’t take their beloved cat, Spotty, with them. So, they did the next best thing, i.e., keeping the cat with their parents. 

But little did this user know that their parent would give away their cat to someone else without even telling them. Fortunately, Nexerxe set up a fund to get back her furry friend. And at last, this broke college kid reunited with their cat.
