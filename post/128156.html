This Adorable Munchkin Kitten Sleeps Like A Human

None of you would deny that cats are ruling the Internet today. Everywhere you see, you find funny and adorable cat photos with millions of likes and comments on them. For some cats, they find a cozy corner of a blanket and sleep quietly. While some would stretch on the couch and sleep shamelessly. On the other hand, we have this cute kitten who has an odd posture of sleeping. It almost looks like this one sleeps in a why that most humans would.

Most cats like to curl up or cuddle with their human while sleeping. This one is a bit odd, who does not need any support to sleep.

Chata lives in Japan and is 2 and a half months old, but she’s already gone viral on the internet because of the way she sleeps.  She’s too cute!
