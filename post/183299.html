33 Movie Plot Twists That Seriously Messed Us Up

Remember when you saw these movies for the first time? Did your mind go blank right out of your skull?

Throw on that helmet again, because we’re going to destroy your mind!

 

Movies have complex ideas, complicated characters, and amazing storylines.

Most of the time, at least.

 

The storyline of a movie can send you on a roller coaster of emotions.

Including plots that tend to keep us on our toes.

 

BEWARE OF SPOILERS!

Don’t worry, the name of the film will be in the title, so you’ll get a lot of warnings before you see the movie.

 

1. Moon.

Sam Bell is a guy who discovers a bunch of clones of himself on his Moonbase.

He doesn’t know that he’s a clone and that the real Sam Bell is with his family.

 

2. Spider-Man: Homecoming

The best twist in a movie so far. Adrian Toomes is Peter’s prom date.

Adrian can discover Peter’s secret identity in about 5 seconds.

 

3. The Usual Suspects.

In the end, we find out that Verbal is Keyser Sze and Kujan.

It was too late. Keyser was already gone.

 

4. Orphan.

Throughout the movie, you’re wondering what’s going on with Esther. Is she crazy? Is she an evil child? Nope!

She was a murderer in disguise. Mind is blown.

 

5. Murder on the Orient Express.

The audience likes mystery movies. Who was the murderer? He was away at the time and it couldn’t have been him.

When the answer is everyone is the murderer, things get interesting.

 

6. American Psycho.

The ending of this movie was crazy. Paul’s apartment was clean. There is a chance that the shooting with the cops never happened.

Is Paul still alive? How could that happen?

 

7. Perks of Being a Wallflower.

It was like a gut punch. Charlie realized that his aunt had sexually abused him when he was a child.

What a terrible twist.

 

8. The Departed.

Colin thinks he has gotten away with everything. Everyone who could’ve outed Billy is dead. He’s at home.

He was shot by Staff Sgt. Sean Dignam.

 

9. Shutter Island.

It was one of the best twists ever.

Teddy, the main character in the movie, discovers he is a mental patient after the movie is over.

 

10. Whiplash.

After all the torture and abuse, he gets his comeuppance. Andrew testified against him.

We thought that Andrew had testified against him. We were wrong.

 

11. Black Swan.

When she’s onstage, she realized that she actually stabbed herself, not the other way around.

Don’t go for ballet, that’s for sure.

 

12. Toy Story 2.

Zurg being Buzz’s father was a cool twist, but it was probably a Star Wars joke.

Stinky Pete was the villain? Some kids were messed up by that twist.

 

13. The Sixth Sense.

The thing that launched M is possibly the greatest and most prolific twist of all time. Night Shyamalan has a career. You know it, come on.

Bruce was dead the whole time.

 

14. Get Out.

Chris was trying to get out of the house when Rose held his keys up.

Chris and the rest of us knew what was going on. She was a part of the scheme all the time.

 

15. La La Land.

At the end of the movie, we get a beautiful, dreamy scene of Mia and Sebastian living happily ever after.

Mia is shown with another guy. It was rude.

 

16. Ex Machina.

There was a mind-blower at the end of the movie.

The helicopter that was meant for her creator was used to help her escape from the house. What a twist.

 

17. The Prestige.

Robert Angier discovered that it was actually two identical twins living the life and performing the seemingly impossible magic tricks of his magician rival, Alfred Borden.

 

18. Atonement.

Briony lied about her apology.

She was too scared to confront them and they both died. Wow, what a way to end it all.

 

19. Seven Pounds.

Ben is trying to learn about his past throughout the movie.

He killed seven people while texting and driving. A powerful message.

 

20. Interstellar.

Mann lied to Cooper so he could murder him.

He wanted to cover his tracks.

 

21. The Visit.

Look, another M. Night Shyamalan movie on the list. It’s like he’s known for twists.

The mother of the children noticed that the old people they were staying with weren’t their grandparents, but escaped mental patients.

 

22. Psycho.

The most prolific twist of all time is probably the last Sixth Sense comment.

You know the drill. Norman was pretending to be his mother all the time.

 

23. Planet of the Apes.

A group of astronauts land on a planet that has been taken over by talking apes.

You can see the Statue of Liberty in the film.

 

24. Harry Potter.

It is one of the biggest blows to Harry Potter fans when he learns that he is being protected by Snape.

It could’ve been better.

 

25. Everything, Everything.

The twist of this movie was mind-blowing.

The main character, Maddy, was lied to by her mother because she never had SCID, a disease that makes you stay inside your whole life.

 

26. Fight Club.

The way everything comes together in this movie is amazing.

The narrator thinks that Tyler Durden is crazy until he learns that he and Tyler are the same person.

 

27. The Uninvited.

The main character in this movie, Anna, realized that her sister had actually died in a fire many years ago.

She was hallucinating all the time.

 

28. Saw.

There is a dead body next to the other two guys who are trying to escape the dangerous game.

The dead body is revealed to be Jigsaw in the end.

 

29. Now You See Me.

Dylan was supposed to be searching for the Four Horsemen.

He was the mastermind of the whole thing.

 

30. Snowpiercer.

We’re told in the movie that life outside the train has ended.

At the end of the movie, the people on the train see a polar bear.

 

31. Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back.

The most prolific twist of all time is what I said about Psycho.

Darth Vader told the Jedi that his father wasn’t dead.

The world changed for us.

 

32. Iron Man.

This one messed us up, but it still messed us up.

We weren’t expecting Tony to come out and tell everyone he was Iron Man, and then not see the repercussions for a while.

 

33. Avengers: Endgame.

We’re on the subject of Stark. Tony Stark’s death was something that no one expected.

He was not able to stand the test of time.

 

Which movie plot twist messed you up the most?
