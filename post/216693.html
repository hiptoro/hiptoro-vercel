Parents Under Fire After They Celebrate Their Son's 5th Birthday At Hooters

After a video of a 5-year-old boy celebrating his birthday at Hooters went viral, the internet was in an uproar. The video of the pun-filled, scantily-clad birthday party was posted by Darby Allison, which has caused some backlash in the comments. The only question going on in peoples’ minds was, “Why did they go here though?”

The staff at Hooters was fun and upbeat, and they made sure that kid’s birthday was one he’ll never forget.
