Willis’s family told the public in March that he had been diagnosed with aphasia, a disorder that makes it hard to speak and understand language. At the time, Willis was 67 years old.

Since then, his condition has gotten worse, according to a source close to the family. Now, his family is getting back together to spend as much time with him as they can.
