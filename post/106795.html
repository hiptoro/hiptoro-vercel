These Spoiled Dogs Have A Better Life Than Most Humans

Most of us dream of having a luxurious life. Often when people say, ‘it’s a dog life’ it kind of means that someone is being illtreated. But when you see some adorable pictures of these dogs that we have collected, you would feel inferior. Some dog owners have built a bouncy castle for their dog, some have brought a control operated BMW and one has a personal seat on a private jet. Does this already make you feel like you have nothing at all? Then take a look at their pooches who lead an extravagant life.
