20 Insane Cases Of Cartoon Logic

Cartoons don’t attempt to represent reality accurately, but some of the logical turns they take are more absurd than others. And it’s likely that you haven’t seen the majority of them.

Because of this, the lads at Bored Panda have put together a collection of the best illustrations of cartoon logic, which range from using chopsticks when you don’t have fingers to wearing headphones over your eyes. These are hilarious and frequently disregarded, and they may alter the way you watch cartoons forever!
