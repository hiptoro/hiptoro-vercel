This Guy Quit His Job And Sold Everything He Owned Just To Travel Freely With His Cat

Have you ever read the famous novel “The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari”? If yes, we have found a similar story of a man who quit his job and sold his prized possessions for traveling. We feel like he has got a little influenced too much. But hey, we are no one to judge because at least he is happy with his cute cat.

The name of this man is Rich East and his cat is Willow. They both have been traveling across Australia since 2015. OMG!

Before you ask, yes Rich had a permanent job, a house, and everything that a normal person dreams of. But it seems like he was not ready to get settled for something ordinary. That’s why he quit his job, sold his property, and bought a camper van. Rich and his cat have traveled 5- 0,000 kilometers together.

Willow is enjoying the van life very much.
