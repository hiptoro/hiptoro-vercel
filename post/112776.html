24 Buildings Which People Renovated And Made Them Look Terrible

Most of us were taught not to judge a book by its cover. And also that beauty is in the eye of the beholder. But when an old and stunning building looks so bad after the renovation, we just want to break our eyes. Seriously!

The ancient architectural beauty is something that is worth praising. But over the times, these buildings get worn down due to exterior elements. And then, they have to undergo renovations. We all know this, right? But what we don’t know is that sometimes restoration can go so bad and monstrosities that we just want to travel back in time and stop it. 

To show how glass facades and slabs of concrete have ruined something so beautiful, we have collected 24 pictures. These buildings are the perfect example of how one can damage something magnificent so quickly.
