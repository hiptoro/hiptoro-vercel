Artist Hilariously Sums Up Life With A Dog In 35 Adorable Comics

Gemma Gené is a multi-skilled artist from Barcelona, Spain. She is currently based in New York and is back with her fresh comics. Gemma draws, paints and creates sculptures, but she’s also brilliant with her comic art. The artist creates adorable comics that every dog owner can relate to. The cute scenes show the incomparable bond that dog owners share with their pets.

“I missed my pug Mochi so much while I was at work that I started drawing about him on the subway on my way to and back from work.” That’s how these adorable comics were born.

“I spend all day with Mochi and still draw about him daily.” So take a look at these images shared from the comic and see if you could relate to them.
