People Share 30 Of The Funniest Contact Names They Have In Their Phones

So many of us have thousands of names on our contact list. While some are close to us, there are plenty of others who you barely remember saving. Recently, Twitter user Jennifer Wortman shared a funny tweet saying how her husband still has her listed on his phone as “Jennifer Wortman” that went viral. After this tweet, many people started sharing the funniest contact names they have on their phones. Take a look at some of these hilarious tweets and share if you have had similar stories.
