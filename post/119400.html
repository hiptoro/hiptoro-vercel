18 Situations Only Cat Owners Could Get Into

Unless you live with a cat, you would never understand what it feels like to wake up at 6 A.M. for literally no reason. Not just this but there are many other things that cat parents can relate to. And if you are also a proud car owner, get ready to say “Oh, that has happened with me too!”. 

Surely cats fill our monotonous lives with fun and excitement. But the excitement level sometimes reaches a level where we just wish to sit, relax, and not do anything for as long as we can.
