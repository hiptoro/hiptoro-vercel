Ronnie Turner's Cause of Death Is Out

A few days after Ronnie Turner died, more information has come out about what caused his death.

The office of the Los Angeles County Medical Examiner-Coroner says that Ronnie Turner, the youngest son of singer Tina Turner and the late Ike Turner, died because of complications from metastatic colon cancer.

Atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease was also on the list of important conditions, and his death was confirmed to be natural.

Ronnie’s wife, Afida Turner, told the world on December 9 that her husband had died at the age of 62. In her heartfelt tribute, she said that Ronnie was “a real angel.”
