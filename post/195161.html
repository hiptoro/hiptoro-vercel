20 Times This Artist Showed Superheroes Struggling With Day-To-Day Activities

Did you know that just like us, superheroes also struggle with day-to0day activities? Sounds pretty weird, right? Not anymore because Lucas Nascimento, aka Dragonarte, has used his imagination to create funny scenarios.

Here, superheroes face some basic struggles that we all go through in our everyday life. His comics are hilarious and quite relatable.

According to the artist, he creates “short stories without dialogue with popular characters—so people from all over the world would understand.” Scroll below to check out some of his amazing works.

2.

The artist first posted a comic strip in 2013 and that’s when his artistic career began. When asked how he came up with the idea for these comics, the artist said: “I drew comics—long stories with text. But they didn’t have much reach (they weren’t very popular.) That’s when I decided to draw strips—short stories without dialogue with popular characters—so people from all over the world would understand the stories.”

3.

4.

The artist says that his comic strips feature stories about his favorite heroes such as Spiderman, Batman, and the Hulk. “I like to make characters from different worlds meet. For example, DC heroes and Marvel heroes.” It takes him about 3-4 hours or more to complete one story of his favorite superheroes.

6.

His comics masterfully show the funny struggles of superheroes living regular human lives. For instance, when Spiderman takes off his mask and becomes Peter Parker—just a regular high school student from Queens—he needs his costume fixed, so this time, his aunt comes to his rescue. Or when Batman has nightmares after his never-ending fights with city villains, his faithful butler comes to read him bedtime stories. Even superheroes need to work out to keep in shape, so Batman regularly goes to the gym with his comrade Robin.
