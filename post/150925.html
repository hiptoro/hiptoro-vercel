Elderly Cat Finally Finds Its Way Home After Missing 13 Years

After serching for a very long period, Janet lost all hopes of finding her cat Boo. She gave up and adopted two new cats. She would always pray and hope that Boo had found a new home where she was loved and respected.

13 years later, she received a call from a vet who was 40 miles away in Pocklington, East Yorks. He informed her, that he had a cat with him who belonged to her.

Janet got confused and was wondering which amougst the two adopted cats was missing.

Suprisingly, Janet found her lost cat who had lived her life successfully as a street cat until, she found her way home. Ever since the two got united, the cat has been following Janet everywhere she goes in the house.
