Best Reactions Pet Owners Had To The ‘How It Started Vs. How It Ended’ Meme Challenge (45 Pics)

The ‘how it started vs. how it ended’ meme was first posted on September 23, 2020, when Twitter user Vjillanelles shared their relationship evolution. So in meme years, it’s like 127 or so. However, it is still going strong. It has gained popularity because of its versatility.

Couples had their different versions of posting the meme as well as artists, and other people on the Internet shared them how it started and vs how it ended pictures of their achievements. Now, pet owners are hijacking it. They have shared images of how their dogs and kittens have grown from tiny furballs to majestic queens and kings.

The great thing about these images are that most of them show adopted dogs before and after they have found a permanent home. It proves that finding a caring companion can change a life and bring so much happiness.

“Helping vulnerable animals and keeping pets in safe and loving homes requires a commitment from all of us—advocates, pet owners, shelters, leaders, and entire communities. When we work together under a common cause, we’re both saving lives and elevating our society and its laws to ensure cruelty victims and other at-risk animals receive the protection and care they deserve,” Matthew Bershadker, ASPCA President & CEO, wrote.
