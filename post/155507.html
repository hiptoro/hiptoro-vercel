Teen Reports Teacher Who Did Not Let Her Leave To Go To The Bathroom After She Felt Her Period Starting

Women have to deal with the period from a very young age. Usually, women monitor their cycle to be prepared for their next period. But when you experience it for the first time, it is very unexpected. It can happen anytime and anywhere, and society is  not very helpful in this situation.

 A 16-year-old female teenager is one of many young women who knows how it feels. “My school requires us to keep our cameras on during the entire class. If we need to use the restroom we are supposed to privately message our teacher and ask to leave,” she shared it in a post on Reddit.
