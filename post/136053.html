Rescue Dog Gets To Be In A Yard For The First Time, “Zoomies” Ensues

Like some humans, dogs have to also go through a tough time in life. Not all dogs have a happy start to their life. Some even end their entire life living in shelter in the hope of getting adopted someday. There are plenty of dogs who do not find the right home until, there comes one family that loves them unconditionally. We came across a story recently about Bentley, who is a bloodhound that was adopted by KaTarra Taylor at the age of 4. Bentley was moved from one house to another because of his size. No one was ready to keep a 120lbs dog in their apartment. But KaTarra brought her own place and created a yard specially for him.
