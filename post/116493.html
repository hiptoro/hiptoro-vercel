8 Celebrities Whose Life-Changing Principles Make This World a Better Place

Don’t we love it when a famous celebrity takes a stand for something to bring a change in the world? Of course, we do. Okay, we understand that celebs have a tight schedule and they work 20 hours a day. However, when they try to bring a change into society with their kindness, we can’t help but adore them even more. 

To tell you about a few Hollywood celebrities who are spreading kindness into the world, we have compiled a few images. So, go on, see them all to know which celeb is trying to make a change. 

1. Benedict Cumberbatch is bringing equality to Hollywood.

You would be glad to know that Benedict Cumberbatch who is known for his impeccable roles in Sherlock and Doctor Strange has refused to take any project in which his female costar is not getting equal pay. 

2. Keanu Reeves is secretly funding hospitals.

You would be happy to know that the heartthrob Keanu Reeves is secretly funding cancer hospitals. Also, he gives one part of his salary for the treatment of other people.

3. Megan Fox is raising her children without gender barriers.

If there is someone who is standing strong against the gender barriers of children, she is Megan Fox. She is letting her son wear pink color clothes to break the rules. 

4. Ricky Gervais is working for animal welfare.

If you like Ricky Gervais, you would also know that he is in love with animals. He has also said that he will leave his entire property for the animal charity. 

5. Nicki Minaj is helping out an Indian village.

If you know Nicki just as a rapper, you seriously don’t know how humble and gentle she is. You would be happy to know that this great musician has been helping out an Indian village. 

6. Kesha officiates LGBTQ+ weddings.

Kesha is a popular Tik Tok star and she has openly told the world that she is bisexual. She is also officiating weddings of people of the bisexual community. 

7. Ashton Kutcher is fighting against human trafficking.

The world has become the victim of the horrendous crime, i.e., human trafficking. And Ashton Kutcher is working actively to end it. 

8. Dolly Parton is educating needy children.

If you like Dolly Parton, you would also know that she has been working to educate needy children.
