These Children Are The Exact Copy of Their Parents

Can you imagine being a copy-paste version of your mom or dad? Well yes, It’s absolutely possible. A newborn acquires the most attention of the family. Soon everyone starts matching the face of the baby to one the parents.

We have gathered some childhood memories which are photographed at the same age, looking like twins.

Do you look like a copy-paste version of one of your parents, too? Share your experience. Here are some precious moments from the past and the present to see the difference.
