“Got Toddlered” Instagram Account Shows Parents Before and After Kids

Being a parent drains you emotionally and physically. Everything. This is the gist of the Instagram account known as @GotToddlered, which showcases in a humorous way the many aspects of their parents’ lives that have been disrupted by their toddlers.
