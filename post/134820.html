30 Funny Single-Panel Comics By Joseph Nowak

Do you like funny single-panel comics? If you do, you are going to love this post. These single-panel comics are illustrated by a Canadian illustrator, named Joshep Nowak. He has been doing this work for 8 years now and his fans really like his style. 

“Sometimes there’s a trigger from a real-life event, but often they come from dissecting a situation to find a silly outcome,” explained Joseph. “Today, for example, I thought that summoning a demon must happen instantly on the demon’s side, so what could it be doing to show a total lack of preparation? On the toilet, of course!”
