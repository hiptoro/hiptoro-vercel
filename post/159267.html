“Our Larger Bodies Are Clearly Lacking Representation In Media”: Plus-Size Models Pose As Disney Princesses (16 Pics)

As kids, Disney Princess was a huge thing for us. After all, they inspired us with their charm and beauty. But it seems like the princesses were not representing every woman out there. 

Thus, social media influencer Natasha Polis took the matter to her hand. She launched #PlusSizePrincessProject to show how stunning plus-size Disney Princess would look. 

“We take up more space than most, yet our larger bodies are clearly lacking representation in media. So here’s our response this Halloween: 21 influencers dressing up in our own style as beloved female Disney characters,” she wrote.
