Artist Creates Funny Animal Sculptures From Awkward Pics

Do you love funny animal pictures? We are sure you love them. And why not? After all, there is everything that makes those pictures cute and hilarious. That’s why whenever we see such pictures, we share them with others. 

But there is one Japanese artist known on Twitter as ‘Meetissai’ who has taken his love for funny animal pictures to another level. What he has done is that he has started creating sculptures of these hilarious animal pictures. And the results are very funny.
