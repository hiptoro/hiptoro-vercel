20 Dogs Who Became Balloons And Floated Up To The Ceiling

There are so many kinds of dog trends that are quite popular on the Internet. Whether it is dogs tasting some kind of fruit for the first time or dogs doing video calls with their BFFs, netizens love ‘em all. But today, we will not talk about these trends because we have found a whole new trend. It’s balloon dogs. Yes, you have read it right! 

What actually happened was someone posted a picture of flying Shiba Inu on the aww subreddit. After that, the post went viral in no time. And now, many people have started posting cute pictures of their dogs floating in the air or we can say balloon dogs. The pictures of dogs floating and getting stuck in the ceiling are too adorable and it will definitely bring a smile on your face.
