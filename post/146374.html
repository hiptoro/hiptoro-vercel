30 Stories From Women Who Gave “Nice Guys” A Chance

We all want to date a “nice guy” until we realize not all nice guys are kind!

The way movies portray a kind guy makes us want to spend the rest of our lives with someone like him. Someone who “would never do such a thing.” Someone who “respects” her. But little did we know that some nice guys are the worst. 

Seeing the ongoing debate between whose better, a nice or bad guy, a Reddit user Between3and20eh decided to find out the answer. He asked women to tell their experience who let “nice guy” into their lives. We have listed the best answers for you.
