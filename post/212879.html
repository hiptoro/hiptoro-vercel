11 Celebrity Children Who Almost Look Like Genetic Copies of Their Parents

The same eye color or nose shape may have come from your father or mother, respectively. And nothing about this is shocking. When we are young, we don’t pay attention to such things, but as adults, we could start to realize how much our faces genuinely resemble those of our parents.

Bright Side examined 11 celebrities and their offspring to demonstrate that a genetic copy is not a myth.
