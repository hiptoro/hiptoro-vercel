30 Of The Best Reactions Texans Had To Being Told Not To Use ACs During A Heatwave

Living in Texas has not been easy this year. After all, back in February, Texans have suffered from the deadly winter storm. Since the state was not ready to face such an event even after its own electric power grid was managed by ERCOT, millions of people were living without electricity. 

And now, there’s blistering heatwave ravaging in Texas reaching over 90 degrees. In such a situation, Texans are requested to save energy. They are asked to set their thermostats to 78 degrees during the day and 82 degrees at night. In short, no relief from the heat.
