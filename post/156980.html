People Who Are Friends With Influencers Are Revealing What Their Lives Are Really Like, And It’s Quite Depressing (36 Answers)

The shiny and glitzy lives of our favorite influencers have inspired a lot of us to become one of them. But have you ever thought about what it takes to become a popular influencer? Or have you ever thought “do influencers live a real life?” 

A Reddit user superfuncity decided to find out what lies beneath the facade. Recently, they posted a question to r/AskReddit, asking “IRL friends of social media ‘influencers’: what is it like?“. And the response to this question was amazing.

To show you what it takes to become a popular social media star, we have compiled 35 revealing and depressing answers from people who are friends with influencers.
