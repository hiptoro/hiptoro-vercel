Couple Saved The ‘Saddest Cat In The World’ From Euthanization And Everything Changed

BenBen is probably one of the saddest cats of this world but soon, he became happiest after a couple saved him from euthanization.

As his health was compromised, no one wanted to adopt him. But a kind woman that works at an ER vet clinic took BenBen to her home before his euthanization was scheduled.

That’s when she developed a strong bond with the cat. And now, well, the cat is happily living with her.
