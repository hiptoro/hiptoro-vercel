What Horror Movie Actors Look Like Off-Screen

Many of us have seen horror movies. when we watch them, we not only afraid of the sounds and produced special effects but also the appearance. Bad appearances strike our mind badly. They give us the creeps. We feel the characters inside this are disgusted. But if we see them in real life they are totally different from them.

We have some characters who belonged to picture like this. Have a look at them and tell us how they affect you.

                                  1.  Pennywise the Dancing Clown — Bill Skarsgård

                                                                                      IT, 2017

Bill Skarsgard had made many movies The Divergent Series: Allegiant and Anna Karenina, but he became very famous because of the role the scary clown Pennywise in IT. Bill was so good in his role that he made half the kids on the set cry.

Mama,2013

So next time watch carefully maybe u get Javier Botet in the face of a zombie
