Bachelor Party Crew Finds 7 Newborn Puppies And They Adopt Them All

Bachelor’s party is mostly about boozing and enjoying with friends. But a party crew found seven newborn puppies at the bachelorette and adopted them.

It was 8 guys and 8 pups (one mom and 7 babies). Thus, each of the guys decided to adopt a puppy.
