This Labrador Allows Foster Dogs To Sleep Right On Top Of Her

When Michelle Calicchio and his family adopted Maggie-the yellow Labrador back in 2005, they thought she is the only dog they would be having. But it seems like Maggie had some other plans. 

A couple of years later, she temporarily brought a stray chihuahua named Percy and fostered. After that, this cutie fostered about 60 dogs for the past 12 years. That’s so sweet. 

 According to Calicchio, “I think it’s because she’s warm and she’s soft, but it’s the strangest thing. Not every single one of them does it, but a lot of them do, and independently of each other — so it’s not a learned behavior. I don’t know what it is about her.”
