This Taiwanese Artist Creates Hilarious Animal Comics (30 Pics)

Have you ever seen the hilarious animal comics drawn by BrainHoleSky? Well, you will see them today and you are going to love them. 

BrainHoleSky is a Taiwanese artist who has created a sensation with his great artistic skills. Initially, the artist used to create these comics for his personal Facebook account. But sooner, he has started a page dedicated to the funny animal comics only. 

“I think having animal characters is a good way to express them all. For example, a pug face can express a very sad situation. Or when you see a swelling pufferfish, you know it is very angry,” explained BrainHoleSky. “You can very easily imagine how shocked the umbrella lizard might look like. In conclusion, I often use animal symbols for emotions.”
