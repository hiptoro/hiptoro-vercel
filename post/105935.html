This Dog Was Getting A Human Sister, So Her Owner Built Her An Epic Room Under The Stairs

Hello people! There is a story that I want to share with you all. It’s about my pet Molly who is going to be a big sister to a little human. Since Molly is going to become a sister, she surely needs a room of her own. That’s why I have decided to construct it for her. 

Before the baby arrived, I had started making a room under stairs where nothing existed before. Since it was walled-in, I wasn’t sure about how much space is available or how great the room will look. But luckily, everything went as planned and I and Molly both are happy with the results.
