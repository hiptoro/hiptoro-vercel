Baby Bat Rescued After Being Hit By Car Stuffs Her Cheeks With Banana, And The Video Is So Adorable

A baby bat named Miss Alicia was rescued by a woman in Queensland, Australia. The baby bat had a bad encounter with a vehicle and and required immediate care. She was helped and taken care by bat conservationist Denis Wade. Miss Alicia is set to make a complete recovery as she does not have any significant injuries. You can look at her pictures and videos in which she looks cute as hell.

While she was recovering, you can see her wrapped in a comfortable blue towel. In the video, you can see Wade feeding the tiny bat. It is a fact, that flying foxes have a sweet tooth, and therefore, their food mainly consists of nectar and fruits.
