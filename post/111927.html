Artist Volunteers To Take Photos Of Shelter Dogs To Help Them Get Adopted, Here Are His 30 Best Ones

Unleashed Fur is a handle on Facebook and Instagram. The account holder is a professional pet photographer, and have been volunteering his photography services to the Idaho Humane Society for over a decade to raise money and help pets get adopted.
His services initially began with them using his stock photography for billboards and magazine articles. It then progressed to doing photoshoots for articles for Paw Prints publication and taking photos at See Spot Splash and See Spot Walk (yearly events for dogs and their owners).

Recently, they had built a new facility, which was made of the donation collected from public. This allowed the photographer to utilize the space to take studio-like photos of the dogs up for adoption, out of their cages. These photos are more likely to get shared more, which results in dogs being adopted much faster into forever homes.
