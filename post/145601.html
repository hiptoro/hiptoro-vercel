People Are Getting Wholesome Tattoos Of Their Dog's Paw Prints

Get ready to call your tattoo artist because you might want to get inked today. 

If you are a dog lover, you would know how it feels when they are not there anymore. It breaks our hearts each time realizing that we have been left alone. And all we wish is to hold them forever!

Ah, that’s simply not possible because death is inevitable. But what is possible is getting an adorable dog’s paw tattoo. It is also a cute way of honoring our furry angels <3
