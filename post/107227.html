20 Cute Pictures Of Dachshunds Which Will Turn That Frown Upside Down

Dachsund dogs are hugely recognized by people as the hotdog. They are tiny in size and are scent hunting dogs. Some people find it hard to pronounce the breeds name so have given them nicknames such as Wiener-Dog, Sausage Dog, Doxie, and more. If you are looking for a small breed with very little maintained then these tiny pooches are the best choice for you.

They were originally bred to hunt badgers and other tunnelling animals. But over the course of time, they have become excellent family dogs.
