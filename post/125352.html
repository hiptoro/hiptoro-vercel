Woman Installs A Feeder Cam For Birds In Her Yard, And The Photos Are Remarkable

Ah, birds! They are the symbol of freedom. But if you go near them, you might scare them. So, how are you going to get their closer look? Don’t lose hope because Lisa is here to give you some ideas. 

She likes to call herself Ostdrossel on social networks and is fascinated by the beauty of different birds just as we are. To capture the mesmerizing beauty of birds, she uses Bird Photo Booth 2.0. This booth consists of a macro lens, a camera in a box, and a clip-on feeder. 

The birds that regularly visit her garden looks so beautiful. You can scroll down to see their pictures.
