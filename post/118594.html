“What’s Wrong With Your Dog?”: People Are Posting Pictures Of ‘Malfunctioning’ Dogs (35 Pics)

Do you think that your job is difficult? If you are saying yes, it’s because you don’t know the pressure of becoming a perfect dog. Hey, c’mon, don’t laugh, the pressure is real and you won’t understand it. And how can you? After all, you don’t know what it takes to protect the house from the postman. Or you don’t know the effort it takes to run and catch the ball. Phew!

Also, resisting the urge for sleeping the whole day and resting delicious food is something that no one can do. But our furry angels are doing it perfectly. So, yes, they got the most difficult job. This also means that if dogs sometimes malfunction, it’s justified because they are just too exhausted to act normally. 

Scroll down to have a look at some of the cutest dog malfunctions. 

#1 He Collected All Balls He Found In The House And Then Laid Like This For 10 Minutes

These pictures are taken from the subreddit r/WhatsWrongWithYourDog. In this subreddit, people post pictures of their dogs in the most hilarious way. This subreddit has 984,000 members and it’s really getting popular day by day.
