15 Pictures Of Pets Copying Facial Expressions Of Their Owners

There is a popular trend on the Internet and netizens are simply loving it. Do you know what we are talking about? Well, it’s the trend where dogs mimic their owners’ facial expressions. 

It all started when Liam Rice from the Isle of Man clicked a couple of selfies with his furry friend Luna. Then he shared those photos on Twitter and people kinda liked them. Thus, a trend was created. And now more and more people have started clicking pictures where the pet mimics their expression. Cool, right? Of course, it is. 

Liam simply loves animals. That’s why he has 5 cats other than the charming husky Luna. He works caring for animals at the Manx SPCA. Liam is simply happy and satisfied with what he does and says “My main duties at the ManxSPCA are caring for and rehoming dogs in the kennels,” he shared. “Fairly often I get the chance to help with the welfare side and go out and rescue animals such as cats, dogs, and even more wild animals like seals!”

You will be amazed to know that the picture that Liam has shared with his dog has nearly 200K retweets. If you want to see how pets are intimating their owners, scroll down.
