These 35 Pics Show How Dystopic Our Society Already Is, And Maybe They’ll Inspire You To Change Something

How do you picture the dystopian universe in your mind? Is it surreal or is it more realistic? Well, Mark Fisher, the late Brtish academic and cultural theorist believes that we are currently living is a dystopian society.  He coined the tern ‘boring dystopia’  in 2015 which refers to the enforcement and pressure we have to deal with in the capitalist society. There are so many problems in society that we neglect on a daily basis which should be given enough attention.

The sub-Reddit titled ‘A Boring Dystopia,’ shares images of the real problem that exist in society today, and should grab the attention of each and every one.
