Andrea Bocelli And Her Daughter Perform Sweet Duet

Andrea Bocelli – who isn’t familiar with this name. He is one of the finest singers around the world. He is the one who invented the unique blend of opera and pop music.

Receiving a glimpse into the personal life of a talented person is really very difficult and rare nowadays. Bocelli posted videos in which he sings with his young super talented daughter.

His exuberance, pride, and patience is something that is quite noticeable. The video shared is a perfect example of a perfect loving family where the father-daughter interaction didn’t go unnoticed.

Bocelli’s little daughter is a star in the making, and one day we’ll be able to say, we saw her before she was famous.

Andrea is the one who burst onto the scene after winning the 1994 Sanremo Music Festival garnishing the highest score ever recorded.

He soon became an international sensation, holding a mark on the zeitgeist of the world. Celine Dion paid a huge compliment to Bocelli and said –

 “If God had a singing voice, it would sound a lot like Andrea Bocelli”.

A voice like Bocelli’s only comes along once in a lifetime, and it looks as though he’s passed that unearthly gene onto his children

Andrea’s first daughter

Andrea welcomed his third daughter Virginia in March 2012. Veronica Berti and Andrea’s first child is Virginia. He has two more children Matteo and Amos from his previous marriage.

From the looks of the video, it can be clearly seen Virginia is following his father’s footsteps. Her mom, Veronica is always there to support her budding voice and stage presence. The torchbearer, Andrea is slowly and steadily is passing the torch to his daughter. His music is in safe hands.

Fall on Me

In the video, it can be seen that Virginia is singing her brother’s part of the hit song, which was made famous by her father and brother, Matteo. The song was written by A Great Big World, a famous music duo – “Fall on Me“. Bocelli’s duet is on the 2018 album.

Like father, like daughter

Watching the videos and seeing Virginia singing on different karaokes is actually beautiful. It is awesome to see her work with her father expressing such admiration, joy, and encouragement.

The videos take place inside the walls of Bocelli’s homes

Youtubers are ecstatic over this intimate video. Comments received were very promising, –

“As much as I enjoy Andrea’s singing, sometimes watching him interact with his kids brings me more joy.”

“I love how involved her mother is. Lucky girl to have parents who are so encouraging and loving.”

“So much of love in this family! This is success. This is true prosperity.”

Bocelli’s wunderkind daughter sing her heart out, and you’ll be looking back years from now, and able to say, you knew her before she was famous
