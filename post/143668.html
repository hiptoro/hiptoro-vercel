Lost Senior Pit Bull Adopted By Police Department As They Couldn’t Find His Home

It is always heartwarming to see people taking care of furry angels without any selfish motive. Like Ohio’s Kirtland Police Department. This police department found a lost senior Pit Bull and then, they began searching his house. But when they couldn’t find his house after a few weeks, they did the most amazing thing. They adopted him <3

After adopting this adorable dog, they shared the entire story on Facebook and even renamed the dog’s name to JD (John Doe). With the permission of the Mayor and the Chief, Kirtland Police Association did this wonderful thing.
