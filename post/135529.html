Teacher Shares Photos Of A Backpack A Father Made For His Son Because He Wanted To Save Money

The biggest milestone in a child life is to get the right education. You’ll be surprised to know that, more than 50% of the children who should attend kindergarten are far away from it. This is not becuase they do not want to study. But because the society has set rules about how a student should come to school. We give more priority to things that aren’t going to shape a child future.  This story might inspire you and encourae you to look how parents make small efforts for helping children study in school.

Recently a teacher from Cambodia shared photos of a backpack a father made for his son
