Clever Street Artist Paints Fake Shadows to Playfully Trick Passersby

 

Have you ever imagined what the silly shadows of ordinary things would look like? If yes, you are in luck because Damon Blangar is going to treat your eyes with his stunning artwork. He is a California-based creative artist who is surprising everyone with his amazing talent.

For his most recent work, he decided to become a little playful with the street art project. The best thing is that his artwork is giving the ordinary objects a silly shadow that you will absolutely love.

He was commissioned to paint the streets of downtown Redwood City, California. So, he has painted faux shadows near ordinary objects. And this whole thing is giving a magical whimsical touch.
