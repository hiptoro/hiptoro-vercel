17 Unique Shops That Know How to Surprise Their Customers

Going to a hardware or grocery store is not something that makes us happy. It’s the same old task that we have to repeat again and again. 

But there are a few unique stores that have something great for their customers. Like in Paris, there’s a shop that sells colorful toilet paper. And it’s presented on the shelves as if it were some kind of elite wallpaper. And in Salt Lake City, there’s a shopping mall with a real mountain stream running through it.

Besides these stores, there are many more stores that will surprise you with their collection of products or something else.
