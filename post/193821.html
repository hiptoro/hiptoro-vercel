Design fails never fail to amaze us. They also teach us that not every design is a complete success – but it’s not the end of the world if you take it as a lesson and try to improve yourself in the future.

These funny are here to show you, that good designers usually deserve more credit than they get. Sadly, not every bad designer feels the need to improve themselves and continue “blessing” us with more of their terrible designs.
