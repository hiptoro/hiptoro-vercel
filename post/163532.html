Woman Goes Viral For Her Demonstration Of How Eating Affects The Way You Look In Different Outfits

Women are in general told a lot about their bodies. A woman named Clara Guillem from Nashville, Tennessee, shared her pictures so that everyone could see how the body changes every time you eat something. She is a body positivity influencer and has been inspiring people on social networking platforms.

 

“I remember I was in the car driving somewhere and I started thinking about how people only show outfits when they feel like their stomach is flat,” Guillem told Hiptoro. “I was thinking about a video I saw that said people see a flat stomach as an accessory and decided it would good to show how cute outfits are still cute after you nourish your body!”
