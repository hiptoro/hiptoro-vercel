People In The Philippines Take Proof Of Delivery To A New Level

If you are an online shopper, you would know that delivery men take pictures as proof to show that the products have reached the right place. While some delivery partner does this thing as a part of their job. Some people have taken it to a new level with their excellent skills. You can scroll down to have a look. 

One shopper named Joyceeh Epino has even made a small setup for the photoshoot. She said she would feel irritated as she was not prepareded when the delivery partner would arrive. Another reason for doing this is to “spread love and good vibes during this troubled period”.
