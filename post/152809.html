Abandoned Pint-Sized Puppy Chases Cop Down The Street And ‘Begs’ To Be Adopted

If you’re a dog owner, you already know how caring for a pet is very different from owning a toy that you can chuck at a moment’s notice.

While some puppies act all cute to get adopted, this tiny pup was aimlessly wandering on the streets of Los Angeles. And after seeing cops, this puppy stumbled towards them on his tiny feet to get love.

The officers picked him and brought him back with them to the station. And at last, this tiny pup got adopted.
