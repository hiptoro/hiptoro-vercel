Photographer Captures Identical Twins Next To Each Other To Show How Different They Actually Are

Peter Zelewski is a London-based photographer who majorly captures portraits and documentary photography. Recently, he did a series called Alike But Not Alike which captured identical twins together to show how they are different from each other. These photographs documented by him prove that they might be identical yet when you see them as individuals, they have their unique traits.

“When I first started the project, it was without a doubt the visual attraction which I found most interesting when taking portraits of twins,” Peter told Hiptoro. “But, as the series progressed, it was the subtle differences that became equally interesting.”
