Sometimes, it feels like we can never understand our cute feline friends. It’s just that they like to do some of the buzziest things. And guess what? Sometimes what they do does not even make sense. 

Why are we saying so? Well, it’s because if there is a sizzling hot slice of pizza on the table, these cuties see them as a spot to sleep. Or if there is a pile of laundry to wash, cats would see that as a great place for taking nap. All in all, cats like to sleep anywhere but not where they should. 

Oh, no. Does that mean you have wasted your money by purchasing that luxury and compy cat bed? Umm, we don’t want to break your heart but yes, you have wasted your money.
