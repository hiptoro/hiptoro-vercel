Demotivational Quotes That Are Painfully True (30 Quotes)

Tired of seeing quotes that try to feed you? Sickened by motivational quotes? You are not alone! We would rather twist an ankle than read something like that. We will join our forces and find the most comfortable middle ground in these Demotivational quotes of which we have gathered loads, and each of them is as good a mockery as any!

What should you expect from these quotes? Will they take your life force away from you? Maybe they will take the joy out of you. Well, neither! What these non-quotes about life will do is make you laugh in a very ironic way, where your face makes a smirk and you start to feel amusement in your eyes. These quotes are actually inspiration masked in a ghillie suit of ornery words that should be read as irony. You already know that and will read these silly quotes as a source of entertainment, rather than wishing to bring yourself down.

We should stop wasting our time with this gabble and go straight to the real deal here – our wonderful, captivating, and a truly glorious selection of demotivational quotes. They are, as you might know by now, just an iota down below. Do not forget to give your vote and share this article with your friends once you are there.
