This Rescue Dog Has The Cutest Smile In The World

Guys, it’s the story about a cute and tiny dog Cheech. He was rescued from the streets of Texas. And later, he was taken to Waller Animal Rescue shelter. Since this adorable doggo was so small, the staff members of the shelter house kept everyone away from him. It was done so that the dog does not feel intimidated by anyone. But to everyone’s surprise, this tiny pooch was greeting everyone with his biggest smile. 

The director of the Waller Animal Shelter and Rescue told us a few things. “They thought I was insane and that he was snarling, when I stuck my hand in the cage I thought they were going to pass out. He’s as friendly as can be!”
