Just one week before the Christmas holiday, Roberts crashed Clooney’s virtual interview for “Jimmy Kimmel Live!” by slowly sliding into frame until she was shoulder-to-shoulder with the actor during the interview.

“George, I don’t know if you’re aware of this, but the woman sitting next to you…,” Kimmel, 54, pointed out, once he realized Roberts had joined the call.

“Maybe I hallucinated that,” Kimmel joked. “Wow. It sure looked like Julia Roberts, though.”
