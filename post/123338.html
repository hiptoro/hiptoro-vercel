Kitty Gets a Second Chance from Woman After Enduring a Downpour

A kitten was found by a woman almost a month ago. She tells us, that the baby was just one day old and was crawling helplessly in search of the mother.

“He wasn’t meowing when I found him but he was crawling slowly along on his tummy as though he was trying to find his mother. Poor little mite,” said Kathryn Van Beek, the woman who found him.

 

The kitten was so young that the umbilical cord was attached to it. He was brought home by the kind lady and was given the name Bruce; just like famous orphan Bruce Wayne (Batman).

He is now adopted by the family and his living his best life.
