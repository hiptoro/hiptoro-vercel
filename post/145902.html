10 Famous Paintings Improved By Adding A Fat Ginger Cat (New Pics)

Did you know cats can make anything better, whether it’s your sad life or famous paintings? Wait, you don’t believe that, right? No worries because Svetlana Petrova is here with some proof. For more than 10 years, she has been improving famous paintings by adding her adorable ginger cat, Zarathustra. 

Besides this, she has also published the book titled FAT CAT ART: Famous Masterpieces Improved by a Ginger Cat with Attitude. This book features some of her interesting work.
