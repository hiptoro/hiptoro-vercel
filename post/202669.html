This Artist Photoshopped Celebrities Next to Their Younger Selves and the Nostalgia Is Real

Would you like to feel nostalgic? The images by this Dutch artist should do the trick.

He takes pictures of celebrities and puts them next to their younger selves. Prepare to be amazed!

 

The recent trend of age filters is over.

The time machine has been invented by the Dutch artist @ardgelinck, by photoshopping images of celebrities next to their younger selves.

 

He told the website that his work first centered around funny pictures on the social networking site.

“I just hope when people see my work, it puts a smile on their face.”

 

“It’s pure entertainment, and I love it!”

Get ready. It makes the nostalgia real.
