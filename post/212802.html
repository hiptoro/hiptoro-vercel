20+ Funny, Authentic Comics By Polish Artist Verauko That Show How Life Is

Life is a mystery all the time. How come? We never know what will transpire in our lives, which is why. It has many surprises, but not all of them are fantastic. Numerous people have grown up in a culture that constantly creates idealized role models and lifestyles, yet the harsh reality frequently falls short of our fantasies. Just a fact of life, Let’s be honest.

What then awaits you in this article? Here, we’ve compiled a list of more than 20 Verauko comics that might force the depressing reality of life into your face. You can find yourself in these humorous situations.

Natalia Trykowska, a successful Polish graphic designer and digital artist located in London, United Kingdom, goes by the moniker Verauko. This young woman is a rising talent with humor and a distinctive style. She is currently a popular creator. Do you understand the formula behind her success? It involves honing artistic skills, coming up with fresh ideas, and making use of social media’s influence. Indeed, one of the platforms that increased the popularity of her comics was Instagram. Verauko’s illustrations are obviously beloved by more than 62K of her Instagram followers, so we aren’t the only ones who think so.

The hardest aspect of being creative, for Natalia and even other artists, is coming up with an idea. She fortunately has a strong imagination for the visual aspects. She can’t think of any stories when forced to, though. Because of this, the majority of her jokes are about real-life issues, including funny and unpleasant situations that have happened to her. She will so depict actual events in her drawings, and the subjects are unlimited. Verauko is not your typical artist, either. She graduated with a degree in arts from the Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology, and her talent is evident in the clever and meticulous graphics she creates. So it will be a good idea to read her comics.
