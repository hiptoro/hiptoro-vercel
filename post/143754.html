15+ Notes From Kids That Are Better Than Any Hollywood Screenplay

Leaving notes for someone is the sweetest thing you can do. And when they read that precious note, they are the happiest person. The handwritten note becomes even special when you get it from your child. Its because no one know’s how a child thinks. So, when they finally write down their feelings, it feels so sweetest. 

Besides their innocence, another thing that make kids note so special is their spelling, lack of experience, and writing. To make your day, we have collected some adorable handwritten kids note.

Love Hate,

Ava”

5. “Found a note my little sister left my parents when she was a kid. I guess I was too bossy for her.”

“Dear Moma and Daddy,
