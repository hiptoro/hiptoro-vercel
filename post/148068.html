An Artist Carves Animals From Logs That Are So Realistic, It Seems They Jumped Out Right From the Forest

If you are a nature lover, you will definitely love Mori Kono’s work. He is a Japanese-Canadian sculptor who carves beautiful and natural-looking animals from logs. Since you might not understand how amazing his artwork is just by reading what’s written here, we have compiled some of the pictures of his work.
