Guy Gets Mad When Friend Refuses To Buy Him A New Phone After Her Toddler Destroys His, Sparks A Debate Between Parents Online

As a parent, you must take responsibility for all of your kid’s mistakes. And when a kid breaks something that does not belong to them, you must fix the problem. But it seems like some parents are not ready to take this responsibility. 

Recently, a Redditor said one of his friend’s kids broke his cellphone. When he approached the mom of the kid and asked her to buy him a new one, she refused. He asked the subreddit “Am I the [Jerk]” whether or not he was wrong after sharing his story.
