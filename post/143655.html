Lonely Dog Leaves His Backyard All Because He Wants A Hug From His Buddy

Messy, a yellow Labrador Retriever, lives in Thailand with his mother, Oranit Kittragul. And also he improves the lives of everybody around him. Simply ask the neighbor dog.

Audi, the dog that lives across the street (where Messy lives) is generally lonely when his owner goes out. But the best thing is that Messy always goes to hug him.  

When Audi sees Messy, he stops crying because he knows his friend has come to comfort him. 

This lonely Husky and yellow Labrador Retriever has got each other’s back. And after a heartwarming hug session, both the dog goes back to their house.
