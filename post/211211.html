Pierce Brosnan Shares Heartfelt Tribute to Wife of 21 Years On Her Birthday

In recognition of Keely Shaye Smith’s 58th birthday, her husband Pierce Brosnan has posted a heartfelt message to his wife of 21 years, Keely Shaye Smith.

The birthday greetings were shared with the Irish actor’s 1.7 million Instagram followers on Sunday when he took to social media to make the announcement.

In the picture, Pierce, 69, is seen grinning directly at the camera as his wife Keely, who is holding onto him from behind, tucks her face into the side of his while wearing a beautiful bright pink flower tucked behind her ear.

In addition to the stunning photo, Pierce included a sweet note for Keely in the caption of the post, which read as follows: “Happy Birthday my darling @KeelyShayeBrosnan. I love you dearly. So many years of love, life, work and play. Onwards we go!”

The comment section was quickly inundated with additional birthday greetings for Keely from fans as well as famous friends who were quick to join in on the celebrations.

Cindy Crawford, a model, has written: “Happy birthday beautiful, soulful @keelyshayebrosnan,” while actress Rita Wilson commented: “Happy Birthday ‘nutty Keely’ the sweetest ever!”

Actress Lyndie Benson said the following while this was going on: “The most inspiring couple there ever was. Love you and HBD Queen Keely.”

The first time that Pierce and Cassandra Harris’s widower met his second wife was at a party in 1994, three years after Pierce’s first wife Cassandra Harris had passed away.

After waiting another seven years, Pierce and Keely finally tied the knot in Ireland in August of 2001.

Dylan and Paris, ages 25 and 21, are the couple’s children; they are Pierce’s youngest children out of a total of five children.

In addition, Pierce is the biological father of Sean, who is 38 years old, Chris, who is 49 years old, and Charlotte, who was adopted but passed away; he had all three children with his first wife, Cassandra.

Following the passing of their biological father, Dermot Harris, in 1986, the James Bond actor adopted Cassandra’s children Charlotte and Chris.

On a regular basis, Pierce will use his Instagram account to post messages to his wife that will melt your heart.

The Mamma Mia actor just recently shared a photo from his and Keely’s wedding day on social media in order to commemorate the couple’s 21st wedding anniversary.

At the time, he penned the following caption next to the photograph: “Happy Anniversary my love @keelyshayebrosnan. We danced that night and still dance the dance today. I could do it all again.”

The following month, in April, Pierce shared an endearing flashback to the evening in Mexico when the two of them had their first encounter, writing: “Twenty eight years ago this day, April 8th, 1994 we met in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. We married at Ballintubber Abbey, Ireland then celebrated for three days at Ashford Castle.

“8th of the 4th…4884 …my love always, you have made my life one of joy and happiness, light of my life, love.” As if they were unable to continue to win our hearts over.

Ladies, this is where we’re going to set the bar.
