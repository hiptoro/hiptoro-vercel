Couple Finds A Way To Include Their Dogs Into Their Wedding Cake And It's Awesome

If we have a dog, we want to include him in everything that we do because we love him so much. Just like us, there is a couple named Gil and Luna, who also feel the same way. No matter what the occasion is, they just don’t want to leave behind their furry friends. And their wedding is no exception. When they were planning to marry, they wanted to incorporate their dogs into it. But they were confused about doing it. 

It’s because they were looking for something else other than wedding puppy portraits. And hopefully, they came up with a great idea. What they did was they immortalized their dog as part of the topper for their gorgeous wedding cake. The cute Chimney, a 10-year-old Jack Russell Pug mix and Einstein, a 3-year-old Pembroke Welsh Corgi were looking so good on the cake. 

Once Gil and Luna charred the pictures of their cute wedding cake, many other people have also started following this trend. And now there are so many options available for people who want to include their precious pups in the cake. They can either have a dog-themed cake,  ring bearing collars, and toppers.
