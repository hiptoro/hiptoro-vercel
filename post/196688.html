25 ‘Inspired’ Test Answers That Show The Kids Are Going To Be Alright

These test answers will restore your faith in all future generations to come!

We never realize how difficult a teacher’s job is until we see crazy answers written by kids on the answer sheet. But hey, we can’t blame kids. After all, we all still remember the terror of taking a test at school. All quivering and trembling you sat there and either scribbled the answers as fast as you could or just stared blankly at the letters-turned-hieroglyphs understanding nothing.

If it were such a case, you would probably leave the page all blank or, being the good kid that you were, try to come up with a plausible answer for a question which subjects you couldn’t understand at all. The smart kids in the list below fall in neither of the categories. These bright pupils came up with the funniest test answers harnessing the powers of puns and their creativity. Reasons for choosing to answer the questions this way are unknown, but we have a strong feeling, that the hard questions brought out some A-class smartasses.

The tricky question, though, is if a kid answers to a problem on a test in an incorrect but smart manner, should they be credited or penalized for it? Below, you can find 25 brainy and funny test answers that will make you wonder what’s better – the correct or the apt solution.

Now don’t get us wrong – not all of these answers raise this excellent question. Some are just hilarious fails. Take a look at our compilation of funny kid test answers below!
