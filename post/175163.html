People In Texas Eat Pickles At Movie Theaters And Had No Idea No One Else Does

Texas is an interesting place. Not just because the Bugs Bunny’s catchphrase “What’s Up, Doc?” originated in Dallas or because it’s called the world’s live music capital. But because it’s normal for people living here to eat pickles at the movie theaters.

I mean, how can someone eat pickles while watching movies when there are many delicious options lying around. But just as much as we were surprised to know this, Texans were equally surprised when they knew no one else did it.

Maybe you haven’t heard, but apparently, it’s totally kosher (get it?) for people in Texas to eat pickles at the movies.
