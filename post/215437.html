Woman With 11 Kids from Eight Different Dads Hits Back at People Who Called Her a 'Bad Mother'

A TikToker seems tired of having to explain why she has 11 kids by eight different men.

The woman, whose social media name is PhiPhi, thinks that she has so many daddies because of “math.”

Some people can’t stand the thought of having more than one child, but for others, it’s clear that the more, the better.

PhiPhi doesn’t seem to care about the haters, and she once went viral for explaining why she has so many kids.

Taking to TikTok, she said: “One thing I’m sick of having to explain is why I have eight baby daddies.

“I’m gonna explain this one time for y’all so there won’t be any more questions.

“If you have one and you take away one, you have zero. But if you have eight and you take away three, you still have five.”

Her explanation wasn’t very detailed, but it’s pretty clear that she doesn’t want her kids to ever be “fatherless.”

The comments, on the other hand, were, as usual, pretty mean.

One person commented: “Bro they ain’t Pokémon. You don’t gotta catch them all.”

“The real question is how baby daddy number 6,7, and 8 allowed themselves to be number 6,7, and 8. I could never,” a second added.

Another commented: “There seems to be a lot of mathematics involved with your parenting!”

“And did the 8th know about the 7 before him?” someone else questioned.
