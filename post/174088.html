Heidi Klum Reveals This Year’s Costume, Proves She’s The Queen Of Halloween Once More

When it’s Halloween, we immediately think of a legend who rules the Halloween transformations department. It’s one and only Heidi Klum. Since 2000, when she decided to pull off goth Pippi Longstocking till now, she has grabbed all the attention of this ghoulish holiday with her costume.

Again her this year’s outfit proves that she is a queen. Scroll down to see this spooky post that features all her costumes.

Over the last 20 years, the German supermodel has proved to everyone that dressing up for Halloween is an art
