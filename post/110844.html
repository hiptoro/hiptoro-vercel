19 Shameless Faces That Couldn’t Care Less About Their Owners’ Rules

Imagine that you were having your lunch and you went inside the room to check onto your phone. But when you came back, your food disappeared. Or imagine you came back to your house after a long day and your living room is filled with pieces of paper. Oh, no. What will you do now? Are you going to punish the criminals who have committed the perfect crime? Or you will let them go? 

Well, you should let them go because the criminals are too cute. Of course, they are because they are your pets. Sometimes, pets can be so shameless that they are not even guilty for what they have done. But we cannot say them anything because they are our good bois and girls who deserve all the love in this world.
