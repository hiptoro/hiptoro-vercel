Dog Breeds Which Stay Puppies Forever (15 Pics)

Dog owners cherish the way their pets transform into a grown-up dog. But some dog breeds remain young and adorable forever.

Dog breeds like Maltese, Chihuahua possess a tiny size which makes them seem like babies forever. These forever small and adorable breeds are the gift for those people who wish their big dogs could go back to being puppies again.

But no offense, both pup, and adult pooch are too cute to handle by humans.

Here are 15 pictures of some of the smallest furry friends out there!

Chihuahua

They are small and harmless, but they definitely possess a big-dog attitude and feistiness that more than makes up for their small size!

Toy Fox Terrier

Corgi

They’re small but behave like big dogs and are very friendly!

Cocker Spaniel

They’re absolutely gorgeous and sweet with curly fur and long ears that just must be touched no matter what! We can’t help but pet a Cocker Spaniel’s ears — they’re so lush and pretty and are exactly what makes them stand out!

Maltese

French Bulldog

Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

They’re just as adorable as the Cocker Spaniel, but they carry themselves with royal nobility!

Tibetan Pug

They are a mix between Pugs and Tibetan Spaniels. They’re very friendly with kids but also bark a lot which makes them good watchdogs.

Beagles

Pomeranians

Pomeranians are quite possibly the sweetest little dogs ever! And a good dog groomer can turn them into mini teddy bears.

Shih Tzu

The Shih Tzu is the only dog breed that somehow manages to look very wise yet very young and adorable.

Pomsky

This mix between a pomeranian and a husky is a guaranteed recipe for beauty!

Pugs

Yorkshire Terrier

Golden Cocker Retriever
