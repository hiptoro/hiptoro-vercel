Guy Photoshops Tiny Dog to Reflect How Big She Thinks She Is

Artist Mitch Boyer is working on Vivian the Dog Moves to Brooklyn, a children’s book. The book he is working upon is about a big dachshund and his journey of moving to the bog city ‘New York’. With interesting photo effects, Boyer has tried to illustrate his tiny pup into a large figure.

Boyer and Vivian, his pet has moved across four states in order to raise funds for the publication via Kickstart. The book is of about 32 pages with colorful illustration of the dog and the city. So take a look at few images and you will definitely buy the book for your child.
