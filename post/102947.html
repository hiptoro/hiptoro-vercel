10 Dog Breeds That Don't Smell

Dog smell is not a big issue but some people are really sensitive to it. Even if they are not, they just don’t like dogs’ smell. And if you are living with any such people you might know how hard things can get when you really wanna have a furry friend but you can’t. 

Hopefully, there is a silver lining. If the smell is the problem, we got a solution. If you want a dog, why don’t you get one that doesn’t stink? To make things a little easier for you, we have mentioned the top dog breeds that don’t stick. So go on and get one. 

1. Havanese

The national dog of Cuba is very little different from the miniature-dog trio fantastico of Yorkie-Maltese–Shi Tzu. It’s because Havanese is one of few dog breeds that has a two-layered coat. This means that they don’t shed that much. Also, Havanese does not smell so you can one without thinking twice.

2. Kerry Blue Terrier

The Kerry blue terrier is famous for its ideal size. This dog breed is not too big nor too small and looks exceptionally good. Kerry blue terrier has thick and curly fur. So they shed nothing and do not smell either. Yay! 

3. Bearded Collie

Looking for a rare breed big sized dog that does not smell? If yes, you can get a Bearded Collie. Yes, big fur dogs shed a lot but not this dog breed. Their long hair makes them look so good and they have very good temperament too.

4. Bichon Frise

Another dog breed that looks super cute and is best for allergy sufferers is the Bichon Frise. Because of the amazing texture of their fur, this dog breed doesn’t shed and stink much. That’s why Bichon Frise makes one of the best indoor dogs.

5. Lagotto Romagnolo

If there is one dog breed that has reached the status of a famous odorless indoor pet, it’s Lagotto Romagnolo. It is a middle-sized dog breed that has affectionate character, easy temper, and super-cute curly hair.

6. Labradoodle

If you are allergic to dog dandruff, Labradoodle is perfect for you because this amazing cross-breed is a hypoallergenic dog breed. That’s so amazing. Not to mention that this amazing cross between a Labrador and a Poodle looks super cute.

7. Dachshund

Dachshunds are counted among one of the super lively and energetic dog breeds that just love to play. This dog breed does not have much hair on its body. So no stinking and no shedding. Dachshunds like to dig and hunt a lot. This means that the only mess that will be around in the house is mud.

8. Basenji

This amazing and intriguing African dog breed is actually from Congo. The best thing about this dog breed is that it does not have a dog’s particular scent. And this thing also makes Basenji a great hunter. Also, they don’t like getting dirty so you will find them usually cleaning themselves.

9. Whippet

If you think that Whippets are weak and fragile just seeing their looks, you could not be more wrong. This dog breed is very strong and makes a great hunter. Alos, Whippet does not smell and shed as they have short length hair.

10. Schnauzers

Schnauzers are double-coat dogs and do not shed much hair when given regular grooming. This dog breed comes in three different sizes. So you can easily pick one that you like the most. And guess what? This adorable dog breed does not stink. Yay!
