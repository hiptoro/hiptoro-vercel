People Share How Much Their Dogs Shed In a Now Viral “Shedding Dog Challenge”

Every once in a while, new dog challenges get viral and the response of pet parents is truly amazing. But out of all the ongoing pet challenges, there is one particular challenge that is loved by everyone. It is called #SheddingDogChallenge and it was started by one of the Facebook groups. 

This challenge asks people to get creative with the sheddings of their pets. And the results are very artistic. If your pet also shed fur, you can scroll this post till the end to get some inspiration. Also, don’t forget to share the picture of the artist shedding with us that you have made.
