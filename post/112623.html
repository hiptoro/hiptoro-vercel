Pets That Have Enough Love For All

No matter how hard we try, sometimes we fail to understand animals. They are so kind, so selfless, and so full of love that we often end up thinking “do we even deserve them?”. Maybe, we don’t deserve them because we are not like them. Unlike animals, we do abandon people whenever we want. And it’s bad. But what is even heartbreaking is that the list of selfishness by humans goes forward.

Hopefully, we have animals on this planet to create a balance. Animals not just love us but they also protect us whenever we need them. Like once there was a case when an elderly woman got lost in the woods. Then a group of elephants came forward and covered the woman with branches so that no other animal could attack her. After a while, the woman was rescued and we fall madly in love with this great story. 

Just like us, if you are also a total sucker for cute animal stories, you have come to the right place. To fill your day with positivity and awesomeness, we at Hiptoro have brought not one but 20 animals that have done some great things for their humans and other pets. 

Scroll down and see ’em all.

1. Kelsey, a golden retriever saved the life of her freezing owner who broke his neck in a fall. Bob slipped on the ice and broke his neck. The dog also protected him from frostbite by sitting close to him.

3. “My pet has been extremely affectionate with my sister ever since she got pregnant.”
