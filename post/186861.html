34 Powerful Women Quotes That Might Just Inspire You

Women, the silent and meek maids, the unacknowledged grey cardinals with a well-deserved place in the kitchen. Thanks to the fair sex themselves, the times when the sentence above would have made sense are long gone. Generations of people destined to make our world a better place were raised by women. With love, care, and compassion. Women have changed society’s view on many pressing issues. All with love and care. The ladies have made some of the most important discoveries in science, and the fair sex is the most deserving leader of countries. They do it all with love, care, and compassion. Despite being told for a long time that knitting, making sandwiches, and being pleasant for the eye are all good for women, they are still strong, powerful, and intelligent.

The article is a celebration of women and their words are sure to inspire you. It will be enlightening to read some of these powerful women’s quotes. These smart words came from scientists, politicians, writers, and actresses who have paved their paths all by themselves, proving that they and we! Can do it all.

We promise these quotes by women will make you excited about what’s to come, and give you some healthy food for thought. Pick the ones that lit a fire in you, vote for them, and tell the world about them.
