Street Photographer Tracks Down People He Captured 30 Years Ago To Recreate Their Photos

Street photographer Chris Porsz spent many hours on the streets of Peterborough, Cambridgeshire in 1970s, 80s and 90s. Back then, he captured some amazing photographs of people. After 40 long years, he visited Peterborough, Cambridgeshire again to recreate these photographs. Chris captured 135 beautiful pictures and has put them together in a book titled ‘Reunions’, with texts by writer Jo Riley that tell you some story about the people.
