Woman Who Foresaw 9/11 Predicts a Nuclear Explosion Next Year

Fortune-telling is an old magical skill that isn’t taken as seriously as it used to be. But there are a few special cases that make even the most skeptical people wonder if something is impossible. Baba Vanga was one of these unusual people. People say that this old, blind Bulgarian woman saw some of the most important world events in recent years. She died more than 20 years ago, but after she died, many of her most famous predictions came true.

Many people still follow Baba Vanga and wait each year to see which of her predictions will come true. Scroll down to find out more about this amazing woman and what she had to say about the year 2023.

 

Who is Baba Vanga?

Baba Vanga was a famous mystic from Bulgaria who was known all over the world. People often call her the Nostradamus of the Balkans because of how accurate her predictions are.

 

How Baba Vanga got her gift.

 

She grew up on a farm, and when she was 12, a dust storm made her go blind. She said this was the time when God gave her the ability to see the future.

 

The first indicators.

According to baba-vanga.com, she started acting strangely when she was 18 years old. She moved back to live with her family in what is now North Macedonia. Baba Vanga had spent her teenage years in a home for the blind. She started seeing things and having weird dreams, and she could talk to the dead.

 

The prophecies of Baba Vanga after she died.

She died in 1996 when she was 75 years old. By that time, she had already made many predictions about the future that turned out to be true.

 

Seeing what would happen to the Earth.

In the 1950s, she made a prediction that seemed to predict climate change and the terrible tsunami that happened on December 26, 2004, in the Indian Ocean.

 

Baba Vanga foreseeing the Earth.

It is believed that Vanga said,

Cold regions will become warm â€¦ and volcanoes will awaken. A huge wave will cover a big coast covered with people and towns, and everything will disappear beneath the water. Everything will melt, just like ice.

 

The Kursk submarine disaster.

People say that she saw a well-known disaster that happened in Russia in the year 2000. During a big Russian naval drill, the Kursk submarine sank, killing all 118 people on board.

 

The most famous prediction by Baba Vanga.

A year later, people who believed in her said she had once again predicted a big event in the world. It is said that she made a prediction that was very close to what happened on September 11 in the United States.

 

The prediction of 9/11.

It is believed that Vanga said,

The American brothers will fall after being attacked by the steel birds. The wolves will be howling in a bush, and innocent blood will be gushing.

 

Baba Vanga’s good reputation for being right.

These things helped Vanga’s followers believe even more that she was a psychic. A lot of people say that her predictions are very accurate. One researcher, the late Bulgarian psychiatrist Professor Georgi Lozanov, found that she is right 85% of the time! So, what plans does she have for 2023?

 

Earth’s orbit will change in 2023.

Baba Vanga said five important things about the year 2023. The first was that Earth’s orbit will somehow “change.” We know that the Earth’s path around the sun changes very slightly over tens of thousands of years, but a more sudden change would be very bad. If we moved closer to the sun, it would speed up global warming, but if we moved farther away, it would cause an ice age.

 

2023: a solar tsunami is coming.

She also said that in 2023, a powerful solar storm like we’ve never seen before will happen. Solar storms are disturbances on the sun that can spread out into our solar system and affect the Earth. Some people have taken what she said to mean that a solar tsunami is on the way. This is a rare event in which a strong plasma wave moves across the sun and into the space climate around it. It could be very bad for Earth and cause blackouts and technology to break down.

 

Baba Vanga said that Bioweapons will be used in 2023.

Baba Vanga said that a “big country” would test biological weapons on people and that as a result, hundreds of thousands of people would die. The Biological Weapons Convention of the United Nations forbids these kinds of experiments, but there are fears that some countries are still doing them in secret.

 

2023: nuclear explosion.

Baba Vanga said that a big nuclear power plant explosion would happen in 2023. This is a very real worry since the Zaporizhzhya Nuclear Power Plant in Ukraine is in the middle of a war with Russia and Putin keeps threatening nuclear war.

 

2023: no more pregnancies

Lastly, she said that natural births will be illegal and all babies will be made in labs. She said that leaders and doctors will decide who gets a baby and when. The parents, on the other hand, will be able to choose things like the color of their hair and eyes. Baba Vanga’s predictions haven’t always come true, which is a good thing. Keep reading to find out which ones she got wrong.

 

Wrong guess: The 1994 World Cup Final

In 1994, she said that two teams with names that started with the letter B would play in the World Cup Final. She was correct about Brazil, but Italy was the other team. She might have hoped that Bulgaria would get to the final.

 

Nuclear war wasn’t coming, as was said.

She said that there would be a nuclear war between 2010 and 2014, but that didn’t happen.

 

False predictions: Europe’s destruction

Her prediction that Europe would stop existing in 2016 was quite dramatic, but it never came true.

 

False predictions: the 44th and last president of the United States

Vanga is said to have correctly predicted that the 44th President of the United States would be an African American. She also said that this person would be the last president.

 

False predictions: the 44th and last president of the United States

Some people have taken this prediction to mean that serious politics in the US will end and celebrities will take over the presidency. Kanye 2020 didn’t work out, which is a good thing…

 

The future.

Now, let’s look at what else Vanga said about the future.

 

Climate change weapons.

Baba Vanga said that in 2066, the US will use a climate change weapon against a part of Rome, which is controlled by Muslims.

 

Time travel.

Baba Vanga says that people will finally figure out how to travel through time in the year 2304.
