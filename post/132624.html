This Plumber’s Invoice For Fixing 91-Year-Old Grandma’s Boiler Goes Viral

Not every hero wears capes because some of them are wandering around the city wearing plumber clothes. You might think why we are saying so? Well, it’s because recently a plumber has given the sweetest invoice for fixing a boiler to a 91-years-old grandma. 

The name of this plumber is James Anderson and he is from Burnley, Lancashire. Recently, he has gained a lot of recognition after a person shared the picture of the invoice he has given to a 91-year-old woman. 

This is not the first time Anderson has done something like this. In fact, he has established a non-profit called Depher CIC in 2017. Through this organization, he has been helping old people by giving them free plumbing services.
