Photos Of Sleeping Pups In A Puppy Daycare Center Are Taking Over The Internet (24 Pics)

Have you ever heard of a South Korea based puppy daycare center that takes care of dogs older than 12 weeks? The center is The Puppy Spring facility in Gyeonggi-do and it shares cute photos of dogs that are taking over the internet. 

This puppy daycare center is sharing cute photos of the adorable furry angels taking a tight nap. And it is probably one of the best things that you will see this year. OMG!

The Puppy Spring spokesperson told us that “there are reservations for 30 dogs each day”. “We also operate as a play center where children can come in to relax.”
