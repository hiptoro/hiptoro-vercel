Person Shares How Dave Chappelle Schooled A Privileged White Girl On Police Brutality

If there is one comedian who can leave a little social message with every stand-up, it’s Dave Chappelle. This sharp-minded comedian is always loved for his unapologetic sense of humor. 

A comedian Kenny DeForest turned to Twitter this week for sharing a great moment from Chappelle’s show. This moment “changed everyone in [the] room”. What happened was in between the show, a heckler interrupted Chappelle’s speech on police brutality by yelling, “Life’s hard, sorry ’bout it!” 

Well, the comedian took time to respond but he schooled the girl.
