35 Christmas Sweaters That Are Actually Not That Bad

Ho ho ho! Now is the time of year when people wear ugly Christmas sweaters.

Think about the one your grandmother gave you that has reindeer and snowflakes on it and little bells that jingle when you move. The one you swore you’d never, ever wear, but end up wearing because “it’s tradition” at your office holiday party.

We don’t blame you. Those things can be pretty cute. They may be tacky and over the top, but that’s kind of the point. They are a way to get into the holiday spirit and let loose. Relax. Have some fun. Because that’s what this season is about in the end, right?
