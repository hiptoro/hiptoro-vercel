Nap Desk That Converts Into Bed And Lets You Sleep At Work

It does make it tough for the employee to work round the clock. The expectation that employees have with modern furniture at offices is to be sleek and comfortable. The Greek design company Studio NL has just introduced an innovative work desk– a “nap desk” that lets employees sleep at work. It solves multiple purposes.
