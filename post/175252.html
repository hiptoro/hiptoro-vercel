Woman Turns Her Chilled Out Cat Into a Zen Garden

If you are a cat parent, you would know that they like to chill. But when professor, writer, and poet Sarah Holland-Batt’s cat was chilling, she turned her into a zen garden. And this thing has inspired others as well.

Professor, writer and poet Sarah Holland-Batt used a tiny rake and some stones to transform her chilled-out cat into a zen garden.

People, the cat already had big diva energy to begin with. How will I cope with her ego now?

And a thousand apologies to all the other cats around the world whose owners now have Big Ideas about tiny rakes. Only zen-rake your cat if they love it, like Lola!
