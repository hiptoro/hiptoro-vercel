According to Bell, any reward-motivated animal is the easiest to train, including dogs, cats, chickens, and even fish. “Their species doesn’t matter as much as you’d think when it comes to training! The more they want treats or pets or attention or a certain toy, the easier they are to train. My two cats that are obsessed with treats are so easy to train that I can teach them a new behavior in a couple of 5-minute sessions. But my cat that is ‘meh’ about treats takes more time, work, and patience. Helpful training tip: I reserve treats for training time only.
