Photoshop Master Reveals Why You Should Never Trust Photos You See On Social Media

It’s difficult to know what is real and what isn’t on the internet. Millions of photoshopped images are far from reality, and we’re not just talking about fake news.

Take a look at the examples to see what we mean. They were altered by a Weibo user who goes by the name of Kanahoooo, although Chinese social media prefers to call her “Photoshop Holy. Why? She has over 430,000 followers because of her photo editing skills. Whether she’s altering guys or girls to match the beauty standards, or even turning guys into girls, there seems to be nothing that the skilled image editor can’t do. According to MSN, Kanahoooo is a person who makes people look shiny and live up to their high expectations. “Don’t believe the perfect thing you see online!” she says. “If you want to find out about somebody, then see them in real life.” There is a thin line between fact, fiction, and ugly truth when it comes to the internet.
