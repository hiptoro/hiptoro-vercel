The Stray Cat Broke Into The Zoo And Became Best Friends With The Lynx

The animal kingdom is full of surprises. Like sometimes, some new kind of animal surprises us with its existence, and sometimes, these furry angels amaze us with their big hearts and unusual friendships.

This time, we are surprised and happy seeing the friendship of a small kitty and lynx. This European lynx was living happily in St. Petersburg Zoo. And that’s where she met this cute Russian kitten. The cat came there in search of food and ended up developing a deep bond with the lynx.

Now, both of them are living happily as the zoo has adopted this cat. You can scroll down to see the adorable pictures.
