Marriage Quotes That Got Me Thinking 'Yup, That's Us'

Marriage celebrates unabashed love between two people. It’s pure, it’s beautiful, it’s romantic, and it’s hilarious.

Below are some marriage quotes that will make you say “yup, that’s us.”

What’s yours is mine, what’s mine is ours.

A happy wife is a full wife.

Love requires personal sacrifice.

“Do I want to be happy or do I want to be right?” Remember that there’s no hill on earth worth dying upon.

Whatever you do, don’t touch the thermostat.
