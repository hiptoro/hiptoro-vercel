Adorable Photos Of Doggos Sleeping At Puppy Daycare Center Goes Viral

Whether you have recently adopted a puppy or you are a dog parent for a long time, staying away from your fluffy angel can never get easy. But now you don’t have to leave your furry friend alone at home when you are working in the office. It’s because the puppy daycare centers are there for you. 

Daycare centers like Puppy Spring in Gyeonggi-do, South Korea is offering extra facilities to make the pups feel safe and cozy. 

“There are reservations for 30 dogs each day,” a Puppy Spring spokesperson told us. “We also operate as a play center where children can come in to relax.”

“We get the pups napping from 2 pm to 3:30 pm. The cost of our services varies and depends on a few different factors, including the time dogs spend at the facility.”

The employees at Puppy Spring snap adorable pictures of napping puppies and share them on Instagram.
