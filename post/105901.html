31 Differences Between Cats And Dogs That Are Impossible Not To Laugh At

Agree or not but we all are suckers for the never-ending battle between cat and dog. We all have got our favorites and we want it to be proved as superior. But we have still not come to any kind of conclusion. However, don’t get sad because we have something for you. If you like dogs and cats comparison, you are surely gonna love the funniest ‘They Can Talk‘ comics. 

This comic was started by Jimmy Craig in July 2015. He told us “I’ve always loved animals and comics.” He further added, “When I look at animals I try to imagine what they can actually be saying.” This comic series that Craig makes is really very funny. And it seems like he does really thorough research each time because all the comics are different and hilarious. “I think animals, like humans, have their own unique personalities so it’s fun to imagine the conversations they might have.”

Even though Craig makes a lot of comics on dogs and cats, he is petless. He says that he likes both of them “but if I had to pick one I’d say I’m more of a dog person, because… dogs.” He also told us that “My wife just told me we’re getting two cats really soon.” 

Till Craig gets a new pet, you can scroll down and enjoy this amazing comic series.
