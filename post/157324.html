Artist Remakes Famous Movie And TV Show Posters In The Most Satirical And Funnier Way

Movie posters are the first look of a movie. They give you a small insight into who is in the movie, and what it would be like. However, when they are tweaked a little bit for fun, they can have a totally different meaning. A digital artist, from Chicago, known as Doctor Photograph creates these parodies by adding his creative charm to the posters. Scroll down to check out his funny and brilliant work.
