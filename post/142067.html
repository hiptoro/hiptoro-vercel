Bride Asks Guests For Gifts Worth $400, People Are Roasting Her Online

When you imagine weddings, you think of people who are going to attend them for celebrating two people who are ready to spend their entire life together. While some couple has their individual roles to play to pull off a wedding, but often people say that the stress causes brides to be in a phenomenon called the bridezilla.

People like to bring a gift from the newlyweds. But none of you might have ever come across someone who would have demanded particular gifts from their guests. Some couple to like to give their guests a quick guide of what they would appreciate as presents. Although not like this bride who has a demand of expensive wedding gift worth rupees $400.

A Reddit user, DexOrangeCounty, shared a post from Facebook that was probably shared by the bride to be. Let’s scroll down to see what the whole post was about and how people started trolling her for it.

A future bride who has remained unnamed has compiled a hysterical list of wishes for things she wants as gifts from her wedding guests

 

Reddit’s online community ChoosingBeggars dedicated to posting screenshots, pictures, or stories of people who are being way too picky about things. It’s description clearly states what it stands for “a place to shame wedding themes, brides, grooms, wedding party, in-laws, outlaws, guests, Uncle Bob, vendors… you name it, we shame it!”

The bride to be shared a list of the gifts that she expects her guest to bring her on her wedding day. Before and at the end of the list, she mentioned that there is no exception but she would make slight changes as mentioned before.

The Reddit community declared the bride to be selfish for asking her guest for $400 gifts such as branded purses gift cards, and much more.

Reddit users were absolutely shocked, some were laughing about it, and some came up with ingenious ideas of how they would teach the greedy bride if they were to be guests at the wedding
