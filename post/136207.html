New Pixie & Brutus Comic Reveals How Brutus Got His Scars

Every Scar Has A Story

As we grow older, not just we get wiser but we also see the world in a clearer way. However, everything comes at a cost. Sometimes, we forget after paying the cost, and other times, the cost stays with us in form of scars. 

This same thing has happened with Brutus and he is here to tell you the story of his scars. If you have previously seen the famous “Pixie And Brutus” comic, you would know what we are talking about. 

 Scroll on below and check out Brutus’s story. 

Trigger Warning!

There are certain things in life that we’re too young to understand. We get to know about them only once we’re old enough.

Every scar that we have shows that we probably faced something terrible and still survived.

In the case of Brutus, it goes way back to when he was in the military.

It was unlike his regular missions because this time the bad guys were pretending to be innocent.

It was hard to take them down because they seemed harmless, but that day, life had something else in store for Brutus.

Brutus was the first one to sense something was wrong.

He charged the bad guy as soon as he spotted him. But it just wasn’t enough…

He got shot.

But he still didn’t give up and took the bad guy down!

The whole encounter left him injured.

But what shattered him was not the physical wounds.

It was the loss of his dear friend…

But it was something beyond control and so he had no choice but to live with it.

Life is unpredictable but it never stops. It goes on.
