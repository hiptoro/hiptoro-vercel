World’s Sweetest Groomer Caught Dancing With A Client’s Dog

For some of you who have pets at home, it is important that you take them to a professional groomer for their makeover. While some pet parents manage things at home, some simply depend on groomers to do the job they do best. So today, we want you to meet the sweetest dog groomer. He was caught on camera while he was bathing and dancing with a clients dog at the store.

Caballero – who runs PetShop Perrito Feliz with his wife, Gabriela, in Buenos Aires enjoys his work thoroughly. His love for pets and helping homeless dogs find a good home is his soul purpose in life.

“I stepped away to make some tea and returned to find Luis dancing,” Gabriela told Hiptoro. “He didn’t realize I was there, so I took out my phone and started recording. This is just a normal day here.”

Cabellero’s affection for his pups is beyond just grooming them. He enjoys every bit of his job and has happily done it for so many years.

“We are a family dedicated to the dogs,” she said. “He loves them, and they love him.”
