20 Americans Share A List of Reasons Why They Chose To Leave Their Country For Good

There is barely anyone in the world who would deny living in the United States of America. It is the most progressing and economically stable country globally. But they say, what looks attractive on the outside, isn’t always great from the inside. Well, unless you have lived in America, you don’t know the downsides of living in the country.

Recently, some American residents started to share their reasons for leaving their country on Reddit, and soon many came forwards to share their experiences as well. Some of the reasons that were started on the subreddit blew our minds.
