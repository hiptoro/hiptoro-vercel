Humorous Comics With Unexpected Plot Twists By War And Peas (30 New Comics)

Do you know who Jonathan Kunz and Elizabeth Pich, aka War and Peas, are? Well, they are comic artists who are getting popular for their unexpected twists, dark humor, and hilarious characters. 

About the comics, they told us, “Sometimes people react quite angrily to our comics, where all we want to do is heal with laughter. Sometimes people write us strange love notes, which are quite amusing. One time a lady yelled at us loudly that she didn’t understand why we’re doing this.”
