People Are Turning Their Cats Into Dinosaurs And We're Not Sure What To Think

Out of all the pets that you can keep, it’s cats that have the least upkeep. So, if you have a cat or thinking about getting one, you just need to deal with the litter box and feed them. Besides this, they can handle everything on their own because they are self-sufficient. 

My current mini-zoo is just two dogs, who I love to death, but require a lot of attention.

One thing that you must remember is taking your cats to professional groomers to keep your kitties well-groomed.

So I’m not sure I would choose to do it if I didn’t have to.

I mean… it’s kind of cute and hilarious.

Plus, for most cats, shaving down their fur can be a health risk.

Most veterinarians only recommend shaving your cat’s fur if there is no other choice.

If you do choose to give your cat a cute shave, it’s important to talk to your vet first.

A close shave may make the dino look more prominent, but it’s not worth it.

So I guess the bottom line is that you shouldn’t just jump on this trend if you think it’s adorable.
