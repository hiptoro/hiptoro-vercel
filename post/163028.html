This Artist’s 30 Funny Comics Focus Attention On Everyday Conversations And A Job In Retail

Do you know how the lives of people in the retail sector look like? Well, their job is pretty much challenging, and Stephen Beals is creating funny comics to give us an idea of how jobs in retail look like. 

He is the artist that created “Adult Children Comics”. Through his work, he is trying to show the things that retail people deal with: small-talk conversations, dreadful work, leisure, and some fun stuff.
