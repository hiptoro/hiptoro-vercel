Meet Eurosaurus, A Dog Whose Smile Can Infect Even The Grumpiest

We humans have a mix of emotions. Sometimes we are so high on energy, and at times we are so sad and low. When the days are blue, and you have a dog to light your day and make you smile by doing their cute little silly things, it is the best feeling ever. 

Today at Hiptoro we have Jack Russell terrier from Thailand named Eurosaurus who will prove it to you that a dog can bring all the positive energy and happiness in your life. She has the widest and the warmest smile on her face that would instantly lift up your mood.
