Indonesian Artist Turns Viral Cat Photos Into Funny Illustrations

There are hundreds of artists who make pet illustrations and make your lives better by sharing them with us. However, Indonesian artist Tactooncat has definitely found a niche. The artist turns a real-life viral photo of cats into a hilarious illustration full of life and color. A great thing about this artist is that he does not make you wait for long to get a fresh image. Tactooncats uploads a new cat illustration every day. Yes, over 116k people anxiously wait for teh post to come up, and so their day is made.
