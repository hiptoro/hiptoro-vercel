Woman Discovers A Hole Behind Her Bathroom Mirror – Decides To Go In And Finds An Entire Apartment

So, it’s an ordinary day. You are doing your daily chores, talking to your best friend, making plans for the weekend. And all of a sudden you realize your bathroom is all cold. Then you find an entirely different apartment on the other side of the hole that was hidden behind the bathroom mirror. Sounds like a horror story plot, right? Of course! But it is true. 

TikToker Samantha Hartsoe has found this hole behind the bathroom mirror after she noticed that her bathroom was really cold. She said, “it was weird because I don’t have a vent that I know of in there that blows cold air.”
