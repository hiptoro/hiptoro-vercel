20 Times Kids Make Everyone Laugh So Hard

Raising kids can be challenging but it’s never dull. That’s because kids know how to make us laugh so hard. They unintentionally create situations that make them the best comedians.

#1 “My daughter’s reaction when holding her new baby sister. Much heavier than the dolls she practiced on.”

#2 “When your three-year-old tells the man at McDonald’s that his pants are falling down.”

#3 “My Son’s hide-and-go-seek spot.”

#4 Toddler says: “Heck This”

#5 “Pls hang ur wigs where the kids can’t reach…”

#6 “My daughter looks like a mom that regrets all her life decisions.”

#7 “My friend’s daughter made him a birthday card.”

#8 “My son was invited to a birthday party to learn “superhero” moves. Aka jujitsu. He took it very seriously.”

#9 “My daughter trying to hide when she’s supposed to be in bed.”

#10 “Potentially the best answer my daughter has ever given on a worksheet.”

#11 “Toddler was asked to feed the cat”

#12 “My daughter has been picking her own clothes and watching her big brother get on the bus.“

#13 “Took my daughter to a van gogh exhibition”

#14 “I might need to have a talk with my daughter, but I’m not sure what I’d even say.”

#15 “This kid was a fart for Halloween.”

#16 “My friend’s son struggling to pick up a book.”

#17 “I too have a daughter going places”

#18 “My daughter and my puppy fight over who’s going to warm their butt on the vent in the morning. The baby won today. The puppy is pouting about it.”

#19 “My 7-year-old sister loves dinosaurs but my parents got her a dollhouse for Christmas. This is what I came home to tonight…”

#20 “At 5 months old my son already has the best school photo ever.”
