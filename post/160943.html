The Highway Overpass In Utah Is A Major Success And The Video Shows Many Wild Animals Using It To Avoid Danger

The Utah Division of Wildlife Resources (DWR) recently posted a video on 19th November showing Parley’s Canyon Wildlife Operpass and all the wildlife using it. The description of the video said, “It’s working!” the Division shared their joy. Check out the video below, dear Pandas!
