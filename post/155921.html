This Stray Dog Followed A Lady On A Hiking Trip And Got Adopted By Her (21 Pics)

“Great things happen at the most unexpected places”

Seems like it was said for Nikki Delventhal and her newly adopted dog whom she met during her hiking trip. Back in September of 2020, she went on a hiking trip with her mother. 

That’s when she noticed a sweet dog chasing her car. At that exact time she knew, she couldn’t leave this adorable dog alone in this place. So, she did the best thing. She adopted the dog. Delventhal named her adopted dog Camper and both of them seem to enjoy each other’s company.
