Man Comes Up With the Most Punderful Road Signs to Make Passersby Smile

Some people can not understand pun. But that is how it is. Very few people can understand the craft of world play. This one group that we can across on the Internet named, Indian Hills Community of Colorado have been regularly making putting roadside signage to entertain the passengers.

The man behind the jokes is Colorado native and volunteer at the community center, Vince Rozmiarek. He made his first sign five years ago as an April Fools prank and has never looked back. “I put up a sign that really stirred up the area,” he says. “We have a heavy police presence in the town of Morrison, which is next to Indian Hills, and they run a ton of speed traps. The sign said ‘Indian Hills annexed by Morrison, slow down.’ Many people believed that prank, and the amount of attention it brought was really surprising.”

The Indian Hills Community of Colorado have mastered the art of funny puns with their regularly-changing outdoor signage.
