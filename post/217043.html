28 Things You Can Only See in Asia

Asia is a part of the world that has its own climate. It’s a whole new world with its own traditions, habits, and way of looking at life. Are you ready to see people hiding their faces behind panda masks, containers for storing husbands, and a section of IKEA with upholstered furniture? Welcome to Asia!
