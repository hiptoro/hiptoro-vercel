Comic Artist Dogmodog’s Comics Are Full Of Dark Humor And Unexpected Twists (20 Pics)

Dogmodog is back with hilarious comic strips full of dark humor and unexpected twists. The stories this artist creates mock today’s society and its issues, often making fun of addiction to technology, religion, social media, and many more topics that concern us today.

The artist already has over 37k followers on Instagram, and I’m pretty sure you’ll join them after reading his comics.

The artist said in an interview that he makes the webcomics to cope with growing older and to have a creative outlet in a world of 40-hour workweeks.
