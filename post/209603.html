17 Smol Kittens Hanging Out in Pockets

Cats are completely obsessed with cosy places, as anyone who has ever adopted one will attest. They can be found in your cabinets, drawers, and storage spaces. They might even be found dozing off under a sofa. They will be wrapped up in the blanket in the morning during the winter. Their absolute favourite places to be are under blankets. We’ve heard countless stories of cat owners searching every inch of their home for their missing cat only to discover their little kitty curled up under a blanket. It is absolutely the cutest thing ever. It only serves to highlight how affectionate cats are and how much they enjoy warm environments.

When cats are young, it is simpler for them to smuggle themselves into cosy and compact spaces. The sweetest creatures to be around are tiny kittens. They are so tiny that they could fit anywhere, which makes it sometimes so difficult to find them. They thus choose locations that are barely visible to our unaided eyes. Fortunately, they occasionally choose to snuggle up in our clothes, allowing us to take them with us wherever we go. Anyway, we’ve gathered 17 pictures of cats who chose to jump into their owners’ pants pockets. For a closer look at all the cuteness, scroll down!
