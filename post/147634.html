Photographer Captures Tiny Bird Using A Flower Petal As Her Bathtub

“This was really a once-in-a-lifetime moment.”

This is a little shoutout to a talented wildlife photographer, Rahul Singh who has clicked some really stunning wildlife pictures. Like rhinos, elephants, monkeys, deers, and other animals. And this time, he has amazed us with his once-in-a-lifetime capture of a tiny bird using flower petals as her bathtub. That sounds so adorable. 

Scroll down to have a look.

This is the moment that left him stunned.

“I visited a place where there were bushes of these ornamental bananas to take photos of sunbirds sucking nectar from it.”

“Everything was going as usual when, suddenly, I was shocked that the crimson sunbird started taking a bath in the water stored in the banana flower petal.”

After filling up on nectar, the mini songbird cooled down in the petal ‘bathtub’.

“I was literally stunned to see this unusual behavior, I kept my camera’s shutter button pressed as the bird took her bath.”

“This was really a once-in-a-lifetime moment,” said Rahul. “It’s amazing how nature can surprise us.”
