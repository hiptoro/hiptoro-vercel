Japanese Artist Creates Hats For Cats Made From Their Own Hair

How to know if a cat lives in a house? It’s simple. You will see cat fur everywhere. EVERY FREAKING WHERE. No matter how much time you clean your house, you will find cat fur in just a few seconds. And all the cat owners are literally irritated with the amount of fur their cute feline sheds. 

But there is a Japanese couple who have done something great with the cat fur. This couple has used the cats’ own shed hair to make a funny hat for them. We are talking about the great photographer Ryo Yamazaki and his wife Hiromi. They have been making these funny hats for quite some time. And the best thing is that there three handsome models Mugi, Nyaa, and Mar are loving it.
