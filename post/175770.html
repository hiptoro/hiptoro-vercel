Single Mom Demanded the Instructor to Teach Her Son Swimming

Being a parent is always difficult, and it’s even harder when you’re a single parent. You want the best things for your child and everyone understands that. But it doesn’t mean that you can be rude to others for no reason.

A South Indian swimming instructor was very kind, as she voluntarily spent her spare time teaching children to swim for free. However, the mother did acknowledge the instructor’s kindness, the way it should be. The instructor shared some screenshots of the conversation between her and the single mother, who unreasonably demanded free and private lessons for her son.

From lawsuits to blaming the world and even threatening the life of her son, the rude mom tried, by all means, to force the teacher to give her what she wanted.

It began with a normal conversation.

The swim teacher, known as Reddit user u/pretent_its_witty, referred to the single mother as “Auntie.” In India, this is the word for women to call their seniors.
