'Oh How My Heart Aches': tWitch's Wife Allison Holker Shares First Post Husband's Death

Allison Holker is back on social media after the death of her husband, Stephen “tWitch” Boss. In her first post since his death, she paid tribute to him.

On Wednesday, the dancer posted a picture of the two of them to Instagram with the words,

My ONE and ONLY Oh how my heart aches. We miss you so much.

Her outfit looks like what she was wearing in the last video of them dancing, which she posted on December 11, two days before he died.

The Los Angeles County Medical Examiner says that Boss took his own life when he was 40 years old.
