Dog Always Brings Her Blind Chicken Sister Her Favorite Toys. They Are Inseparable

Since we take our senses for granted, we don’t really understand their importance. But luckily, animals aren’t like us. Not just they are very proud of their senses but also become assistant for furry friends who are not capable of using their own seeing or hearing senses. 

Like this pit bull, named Gracie, who is taking care of her blind chicken sister, Peri. The chicken lost her seeing sight at the age of three and after that, Gracie started taking care of her. Such a sweetheart!

Their owner told us  “the two animals accompany each other in all their activities. They enjoy sunbathing together, bathing and even helping each other when it’s time to take their feather medicine. What began as compassion made them an inseparable pair.”
