Venomous Snake Found Curled Up Inside Teenager’s Asthma Inhaler

A few days back, a teen girl was bringing her washing inside her house when she notices a red-bellied black snake slither out of the clothes. This incident took place in Bli Bli, Queensland Australia.

She wasn’t sure how harmful the reptile can be. Thus, she immediately called a snake catcher team. They carefully removed the reptile from her home and moved it to its natural habitat.

 

It’s one of the most incredible places we have ever found a snake before. I couldn’t imagine what it would be like picking it up and discovering it in there. They are just exploring, seeking food this time of year before it gets cold again.
