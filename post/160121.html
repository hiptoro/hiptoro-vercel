20 Things That May Seem Strange, But Are Completely Normal for Kitchen Staff

Being a kitchen staff is not easy. After all, you have to deal with things that normal people can never think of. Like cleaning gallons of tomato puree. But sometimes, you can also hit the jackpot and find something really nice. 

To give you a glimpse of how a regular day in kitchen staff’s life looks like, we have compiled some images.
