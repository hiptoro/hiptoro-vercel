I Bring Everyday Objects To Life With My Smartphone And No Photoshop, Here Are My 35 New Pics

Have you met Yahav Draizin from Tel Aviv, Israel who is skilled in bringing everyday objects to life with a smartphone (no photoshop)? He owns an advertising agency called CRACKER TLV where I work full-time.

He says “I’m constantly looking for inspiration. Using my phone, I match images from popular culture and beyond to places, people, and objects to bring them to life, but with a fun twist. All photos are taken in real locations using two smartphones and no Photoshop or editing.”

Here are some of his impressive work.

Creativity and storytelling is my passion and photography is my hobby since I was a little boy. I love it because it allows me to be creative and explore different angles and different stories with a single shot.
