People Post Funny Pictures Of Their Irish Wolfhounds, And It’s Amazing How Big They Are

People can, of course, find Irish Wolfhounds a bit scary because they are so huge. But the fact is that this doggo breed is simply the sweetest and surely deserves a place in your house. 

You will be amazed to know that Irish Wolfhounds have been around since 391 AD (that’s a lot of time). This dog breed is famous for its speed and hunting ability. Probably that’s why one of the High Kings in Ireland has an army of 300 Irish Wolfhounds.
