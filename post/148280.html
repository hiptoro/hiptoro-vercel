People Who Are Friends With Influencers Are Revealing What Their Lives Are Really Like, And It’s Quite Depressing (36 Answers)

The life of influencers is not as happening as their social media account represent. In this digital age, nobody knows the bitter reality of life, instead, believe in what people depict themselves to be.  So, Reddit user superfuncity decided to find out what lies beneath the facade.

So, to find out the truth the user-posted question to r/AskReddit, asking “IRL friends of social media ‘influencers’: what is it like?“. Now Reddit gives you the chance to anonymously post anything, so friends who could not directly criticize their friends got an opportunity to say what they actually feel.
