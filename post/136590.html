Artist Draws Comics That Hilariously Shows Her Daily Adventures With The Dog (19 Comics)

NikNik Ando is a comic artist, who creates comics revolving around her cattle dog and her life with him. She has been really active in creating these comics but didn’t have the confidence to share them with others. However now NikNik has finally decided to share her work.

“My central goal is basic: I like to make individuals happy and laugh. That is the reason I generally compose the sort of cheerful comics that I have love myself, in the expectations that others appreciate them and they can help make a bad day somewhat better.”

To show you a glimpse of her creative artwork, we decided to compile her most loved comics. Scroll down and have a look at these hilarious dog comics that will definitely make your day.
