Woman Takes A Sad Shelter Cat And Turns His Frown Upside Down

It’s a heartwarming story of a woman who adopted a sad shelter cat and turned his frown into a smile. 

One day Tegan went to the near shelter house where she found this adorable cat. She instantly adopted the cat and named him GusGus. Since the cat didn’t have a good experience with previous owners, he was sad. But Tegan changed his life. 

“This snuggly little goofball captured my heart almost exactly two years ago, and I’ve loved him ever since,” Tegan shared. “He has brought me so much joy.”
