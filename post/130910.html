London-Based Artist Draws Minimalist Comics About the Struggles We Face Every Day

London based artist Natalya Lobanova beautifully depicts everyday struggles about modern and contemporary life that we all have to deal with. Her work is simple but strong in expressions. The message conveys very clearly, and her work is highly appreciated. Go through the gallery and you’ll relate to every single thing.
