Chicken Coops That Look Like KFC Restaurants Are Cluckin’ Hilarious

It’s really nice to see that some creative chicken coop owners have opted for a unique design. Their coops look like KFC restaurants a little blasphemous.

 The idea of making their coops look like KFC restaurants have made it clucking hilarious. And these designs have gone viral.

It’s so wrong, it’s right.

It just looks so good, you know? I love the little drive-thru area, where you can get 1 dozen “butt nuggets” (eggs, they mean eggs) for $5.

That is what I call a finger-licking good deal.

As it turns out, a lot of people have KFC chicken coops.

Reddit user bestguguy posted their creation to a subreddit dedicated to talking KFC. The users loved it, and bestguguy said that their chickens actually loved the bright colors!

They even opened up a Canadian branch!

This design is truly impressive. This coop has two floors, windows, and a full door!
