Chihuahua Saves Heartbroken Man's Life So He Saves More Than 40 Little Dogs In Return

We all know what heartbreak feels like. After all, we are wandering down the streets with our broken hearts waiting for someone to heal them. While so many things can heal the heart, in Bobby Humphrey’s case, a 7-pound chihuahua ended up saving his life.

The best thing is that Bobby has now started a little dog sanctuary to save all the adorable dog who needs help. This is the definition of paying it forward!

It’s not just big dogs who save humans lives, just ask Bobby Humphrey, whose little chihuahua, Lady, turned out to be a lifesaver

It all started when Bobby’s marriage ended suddenly, leaving him distraught, heartbroken, and even with thoughts of taking his own life.

“I lost faith in humanity. I got weak. I wanted to end it. One person, above all, Connie, kept me on this planet. True friend that was always there. In fact, she wouldn’t leave me alone long enough to do what I wanted to do. She literally just wouldn’t go away no matter how much I told her to. She lost to that before and wasn’t going to lose me,” said Bobby.

That’s when his friend, Connie, asked him to look after her little chihuahua, Lady, and it changed Bobby’s entire life

“Connie admitted that she was very protective of both her and her son and that she didn’t really like people. Especially men. So, honestly, I just steered clear of her until this point,” Bobby said of Lady.

With Bobby still struggling to cope, it was like little Lady sensed it and began showering him with loving cuddles

That’s when Connie was able to take Lady back, and Bobby decided he wanted to do something more to help little dogs

And so Bobby’s rescue, Big Guy, Littles World Sanctuary was born…
