76-Year-Old Cher, Confirms Her Relationship With Toyboy Producer, 36

Cher’s relationship with toyboy music producer Alexander Edwards was confirmed on Twitter on Saturday when she posted a picture of her man with a heart emoji next to it.

In a series of messages, the 76-year-old singer said that her 36-year-old boyfriend treats her “like a queen.”

The singer, who has already told her family about Alexander, didn’t care about the 40-year age difference and said: ‘Love doesn’t know math.’

A fan asked her to confirm that she was dating him, and she did so with a series of emojis. She also said that they met for the first time last month at Paris Fashion Week.

Cher replied to trolls by writing: ‘I’m Not Defending us. Haters are Gonna Hate…Doesn’ Matter That we’re Happy & Not Bothering Anyone’.

Alexander is best known for dating Kanye West’s ex-girlfriend, Amber Rose, 39, with whom he has a son, Slash Electric Alexander Edwards, who is 3 years old.

Back in August, Amber said that the producer cheated on her with 12 different people, and he later admitted it on social media.

But Cher didn’t care about what fans thought when she said: ‘I’m in love, not blinded by it’.

The former stripper and glamour model also has a nine-year-old son named Sebastian with the 35-year-old rapper Wiz Khalifa.

In August, Amber posted a statement on Instagram about how upset she was. ‘I’m tired of getting cheated on and being embarrassed behind the scenes.

‘All 12 of y’all bums (the ones that I know of there’s probably more) can have him’ she continued. ‘Y’all very much knew he was in a relationship with a baby and y’all decided to f**k him anyway. I saw all the texts and DM’s. Y’all were well aware but y’all don’t owe me any loyalty so it’s whatever.’

A few days later, Alexander admitted to cheating on her and said that cheating was his “true nature,” even though he “loved” her.

Alexander is the Vice President of Def Jam’s Artists and Repertoire Department, which has signed Rihanna, Justin Bieber, 2 Chainz, DMX, and Jhene Aiko, among others.

Alexander was born in 1986 in Oakland, California, and he has been on an episode of “Keeping Up with the Kardashians.”

Cher has been divorced twice and has a 53-year-old son, Chaz Bono, with her late ex-husband, Sonny Bono. Sonny Bono died in 1998 at age 62 from injuries he got when he skied into a tree.

She later married singer Gregg Allman from 1975 to 1978. He died in 2017 at age 69. Cher and Gregg have a 46-year-old son named Elijah Blue Allman.

Cher has said in the past that she dates younger men, like Ron Zimmerman, who is 12 years younger than her, Tom Cruise, who was 23 when she was 38, and Richie Sambora from Bon Jovi, who is 13 years younger than her.
