20 Pics Of Great Danes Who Have No Idea How Big They Are

What comes to your mind when someone says “Great Dane”?

Okay, so for some people Great Dane can be a little intimidating and scary because they are so big (so freaking big). But C’mon, they are not scary. They are just a little bigger doggo who needs all your love, attention, care, time, and of course, food. How can we forget the food? 

People quickly feel intimidated when they see huge dogs like Pitbulls and Great Danes. It’s because they think that big dogs=big danger. But this is totally wrong. Actually it should be something like huge dogs=huge heart and more cuddles. Because yes, your big furry friend would always be with you and can, in fact, very well protect you from dangerous things. 

Try and understand that the bigger size of a dog does not mean that it has temperament issues. It only shows that this big furry friend has a heart as big as the universe.
