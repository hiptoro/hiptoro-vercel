Here’s How The Oval Office Designs Changed Over The Last 100 Years

The most important room in the White House has to be the Oval Office. It is where the U.S President spends most of his time, talking and meeting people. Over these years, many presidents have come and gone. All, they have done to add a bit of a personal touch to the Oval Office remains for their tenure of their service. The next one comes, this process goes on and on. Recently, The American House Shield shared 20 imagines of the Oval Office and how it has transformed from one President to another. Starting with Willian Howard Taft and ending at current President of the United States of America, Joe Biden. Scroll down to check out the gallery.
