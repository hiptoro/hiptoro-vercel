This Elderly German Couple Steals The Show Every Time They Go Out (30 Pics)

While some people are scared of growing old, some people want to age like a fine wine just like Berlin fashionistas Britt Kanja and Günther Krabbenhöft. This cute couple is giving us all some major fashion goals and are stealing the show every time they walk out. 

If you think that old people don’t follow fashion trends, Günther and Britt are going to prove you wrong. Not just they closely follow fashion trends but wear impeccable outfits. If you want to see their sense of style, stay tuned. 

P.S. You might want to steal some of their clothes because they are too stunning

Günther went viral a couple of years ago and told Süddeutsche Zeitung  “since then, I’ve been able to skip my other fitness program, I just need a little strength training on the side.”

“I would have liked that, yes. But I wasn’t someone who rebelled, I did what my parents told me to do.” Instead, Günther became a chef. “It was hard for me at first, but it was okay. A completely normal job.”

“In Hanover, where I grew up, we used to run around in miniskirts. Then the miniskirt was just coming out for men. With Roman sandals, too! But we quickly let that go. When I came to Kreuzberg over 30 years ago, I mainly dressed in black in the typical alternative look here. But always special with a personal twist.”
