Wholesome Photos Of Ingo The Dog With His Owl Friends

We always like cute and unique friendship stories that can move mountains and melt hearts. Be it a dog getting along with a cat or a tiger deciding to befriend a deer that’s served him as dinner, animal friendship is really very strange. And this time we have brought a whole new friendship story. It’s a story between a shepherd dog Ingo and an owl Poldi. Before we go further, we want to tell you one thing. It’s that the owl and the dog look super cute together. And the pictures are going to bring a big smile on your face. 

Ingo and his friend are active members of Tanja Brandt’s photography club. It’s because the feathered & furry beauties are perfect to portray unexpected and beautiful moments of different species. “I’ve always loved animals, since I was a baby, unlike the rest of my family”- said Tanja to Bored Panda in 2015. “When I was little, I found every sort of pet and then hid it from my parents. I love the beauty, power, loyalty, courage, and friendship of animals. Many people could learn from them.”
