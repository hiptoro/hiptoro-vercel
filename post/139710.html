Chihuahua That Can’t Walk Becomes Best Friends With Pigeon That Can’t Fly, And Their Photos Are Adorable

You can never be sure about dogs behaviour. They keep on surprising us with their kindness and attitude. Who would have thought a dog to be friends with a pigeon who cannot fly.

Well, this story is heart touching and unique in its own way. We would like you to meet Herman and Lundy who have an adorable and inseparable bond.  Lundy, a Chihuahua puppy who cannot walk became best friends with Herman, the pigeon that cant fly. This rare friendship is so pure, that it will make you want to believe in kindness and pure love.

These guys met each other at the Mia Foundation, a rescue organization in, New York. It is a non-profit organization that support animals with disabilities.
