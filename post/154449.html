Uncle Refuses To Eat A Meal Without Meat At His Vegan Niece’s Wedding, Stirs Up Family Drama

Planning a wedding is one of the most daunting tasks in the world. Deciding on the venue, planning the menu, dress and decor take months of preparation.

A vegan bride to be posted before her wedding to make it clear to her guests that the menu at the wedding will be vegan. However, one of her uncles refused to agree to the situation and forced that according to traditions the wedding should have meat on the menu.  The drama at the wedding escalated a bit more than what the bride had expected. So, she decided to ask people whether it was right or wrong for her uncle to raise such a demand.

A vegan bride-to-be has organized a food catering for her wedding celebration, but her uncle refused to eat a vegan meal because of “cultural reasons”

The bride said “cultural reasons” were not a dietary requirement and that the newlyweds wouldn’t change the menu because of the uncle’s stubbornness
