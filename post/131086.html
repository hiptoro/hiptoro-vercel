Dog Wears Camera That Gets Triggered Whenever He Gets Excited According To A Heart Rate Monitor

Have you ever wondered what it would be like if your dog has the power to click a picture. We all love dogs, as much as you do. They are your life and probably your phone gallery has more pictures of them than your own.

Nikon has come up with a brilliant innovation, where a dog can now possibly click a picture. Yes, this is possible by monitoring a dogs heartrate. When a dog sees a thing and gets excited about it, the camera gets triggered. The dog is equipped with a heart rate monitor around its neck and the camera is attached to the chest with a harness.
