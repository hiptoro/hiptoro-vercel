26 Hardworking Dogs Who Are "Employee Of The Month" Contenders

Dogs were the first animals to be domesticated. This was 12,000 years ago. Since then people have been using the dogs for various tasks and who knew that they can excel at everything. Their loyalty, intelligence, and compassion can make them great employees.

They always exhibit such quality which never ceases to amaze us. We are surprised by the resourcefulness and wit of the dogs and wonder what if they were awarded “Employee Of The Month”.

So here are some photos to prove that a dog can be an admirable employee.
