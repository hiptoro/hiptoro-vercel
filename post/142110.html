Dog Breeds That Are Extremely Clingy

You might have heard it a lot of times, so we thought of saying it once more- dogs are clingy. Phew! See, we said it. Dogs are super clingy that they can’t stay away from their owners even for a second. 

But which dog breeds are actually clingy? Do you know? Well, we have made a list of dog breeds who are clingest of all. So, think twice before bringing these cuties to your home because they won’t leave you. LIKE NEVER. 

1. Pugs: Picture says it all!

This is a pup who can ignore playtime to be next to you. Want a cuddle buddy who wouldn’t stop? Get a Pug!

2. Maltese

Want to know what their favorite place is? Right next to you. They will whine if they are left alone for a long period of time. But,  they also make a perfect long-term companion!

3. Border Collie

They might not only try to follow you but also herd you. Well, it is a part of their nature. Can’t complain about that now, can we?

4. Shetland Sheepdog

A sheepdog that would also love to keep an eye on everything you do. Sheltie is a true velcro, an excitable pup who will instantly fall in love with your family.

5. German Shepherd: The guardian!

They love guarding because they love humans. This dog will be with you 24/7.

6. Golden Retriever

So sweet, so gentle, and oh so caring! I Will be wanting to do whatever you are doing.

7. Doberman Pinscher

Looking out for danger in order to protect you from it. Will also want to be with you inside the house cuddling and snuggling all day long!

8. Vizsla: clingiest of em’ all!

This Hungarian breed is attached to the owner, like in a literal way! Will be the happiest when with you.

9. Italian Greyhound

These timid little babies are very playful and cheerful. All they want to do is please you. Extremely dependent on their owners.

10. Labrador Retriever

Loves curling up with you on the sofa, on the bed, in the kitchen, basically everywhere! Also loves a lot of pampering. Their attention towards their owners makes them very good therapy dogs as well.
