30 Comics Showing The Struggles Of A Girl Born In The Internet Age

Bloome’s comics are full of humor and self-irony – something that the author says helps Hanna a 20-year-old girl born in the internet age cope with her problems and stay cute and gorgeous.

“I have always been dreaming about creating my own world where all girls will be on the same page. The place where you can laugh sincerely or just smile and say, “Hey, it’s me!”,” says the artist. “In my comics, Hanna faces different life situations and her reactions to them are the same as yours – she laughs, feels sad, goes crazy, and sometimes just wants to be a little girl who can hide under her blanket.”

“Hanna is a unique girl who knows how to deal with pimples and stay calm,” says the artist. “She knows what it means to have mood swings or to have constant thoughts about her hair – “should I cut it or better dye it?”
