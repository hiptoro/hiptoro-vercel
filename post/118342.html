Meet Baddie Winkle, A 92 Y.O. Stylish Grandma Who “Is Stealing Your Man Since 1928”

Guys, this post is about a 92-years-old woman who has been “stealing your man since 1928”. Although she is so old, she is on FIRE! She has her own beauty collection at Sephora and she has nearly 3 million followers on her Instagram. 

Oh and did we mentioned that superstars like Rihanna and Miley Cyrus are her fans? Yes, you have read that right! Meet Helen Ruth van Winkle who is not your typical grandmother. She is confident, she is smart, and she has become an Internet sensation.   

Before becoming a fashion icon, she used to lead a simple and peaceful life. She even has a farm in a small 6-acre farm and she was happy with her great-grandchildren. 

And then came the day that changed the life of Helen completely. One Sunday afternoon, her great-granddaughter clicked some of her pictures in shorts and uploaded them with the caption “I’m a bad b**ch; always been a rebel”. “So we decided ‘bad’ — ‘baddie.’ And then my last name was van Winkle so we put the Winkle on.”
