Man Spends His One Day Off Cleaning Forgotten Veterans’ Tombstones, And Here Are The Results

Hello folks, we have found a great story for this week. It is about a man who lives in Florida and is on a mission to clean the tombstones of veterans. While doing so, he is also uncovering their untold stories. He actually runs a full-time carpet cleaning store. But on Sundays, he does this work. 

To clean the tombstones, he first sprays them with water. Then he uses environmentally friendly chemical solutions, and at last, he scrubs away all the grime. He also shares the before and after pictures on his social media sites. And people are loving his work.
