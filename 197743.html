Chonky Cat Finds His Forever Home After Going Viral For Being An Absolute Unit

Fat just means there is more love.

Of course, everyone should stay healthy as it’s the secret to long life. But we are not here today to discuss the benefits of staying healthy. Instead, we are here to take a look at the chonkiest cat you will ever see. I mean, I have seen fat cats but this is taking chonky to a whole new level. 

If I didn’t know this was a cat, I would presume it was a person dressed in a cat costume. Because not only is he fat, but he is also adorably quite big. And we just love big cats. That is why it is no surprise to find the Internet fawning over this chonky boy. You can see the love people have for this boy by scrolling below. And don’t worry, he has found a forever home now.
