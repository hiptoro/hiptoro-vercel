15 Photos Showing the Depth of Animal-Human Relationships

Animals and humans might have different responsibilities and roles in society, but they are very much alike when it comes to their feelings. They can speak in a language we know of, but their way of communicating their love towards humans is showing little signs of them caring for us. Here are 15 photos that show you the deep relationship that animals and humans have with each other.

1. “Taught Chloe to stand on my wheelchair’s foot rests, and now all she wants to do is give me kisses.”

2. “My cat decided to arrive at precisely the right moment during storytime with my daughter.”

3. “Sunny loves her human, Luna.”

4. “I’m unable to have children so my pets are my children.”

5. “My fiancé befriended an overly photogenic 9-day-old baby goat.”

6. “Every morning she greets me by bringing me clothes.”

7. “My boyfriend has never had a pet. It was love at first sight.”

8. “Got her as a present for my 12th birthday, now I’m 26 and celebrating her 15th birthday!”

10. “Found this guy under my car this morning. Spent about 10 minutes hand-feeding him carrots and broccoli.”

11. “About a year ago, I shared a picture of some kittens I found under my house. Here’s an update!”

12. “A bird fell asleep in my friend’s hair…”

13. “17 days after my mom passed from ovarian cancer, her old Irish Wolfhound followed in tow to be with her. I miss them both so much.”

14. “My friend has pet squirrels.”

15. “Cheerio was cold after her bath. I told her that she could share my shirt until she warmed up. She spent the next hour curled up inside my shirt.”
